# PUBLIC NOTICE 7101

## TREASURY INCIDENT PUBLIC REPORT

### Public Affairs Release

CLASSIFICATION: `L0`  
STATUS: `PUBLIC RELEASE`  
AUTHORITY: `BLUCOR`  
AUTHORIZED BY: `PUBLIC AFFAIRS BUREAU`

---

# OFFICIAL STATEMENT

The [[1102_BluCor_Administration_Charter|BluCor]] Strategic Coalition confirms that a robbery occurred at the [[1302_GrCor_Reserve_and_Finance_Charter|GrCor]] & Trust Reserve Bank located within SpawnTown during the late-night hours of 2025-08-02.

The incident involved unauthorized access to the facility through a structural opening located along the side service area of the building. Unknown individuals entered the reserve area, accessed protected storage, removed a significant quantity of reserve material, and departed prior to full containment by security personnel.

The Coalition stresses that core financial continuity remains stable.

Public reserve operations remain functional.

---

# INCIDENT SUMMARY

Initial review indicates that the perpetrators gained access during a period of reduced public activity.

Current findings suggest the suspects possessed substantial familiarity with:

- Facility movement patterns
- Side-access structures
- Vault-adjacent routes
- Internal storage timing
- Security response windows
- Staff workflow

While the investigation remains active, the current public assessment supports the likelihood of internal cooperation, negligent oversight, or direct procedural compromise among individuals associated with [[1302_GrCor_Reserve_and_Finance_Charter|GrCor]] facility operations.

---

# FACILITY STATUS

The [[1302_GrCor_Reserve_and_Finance_Charter|GrCor]] & Trust Reserve Bank has been placed under temporary Coalition supervision pending completion of structural, financial, and personnel review.

Current actions include:

- Securing remaining reserves
- Reviewing vault integrity
- Inspecting damaged access points
- Auditing internal ledgers
- Reviewing personnel schedules
- Installing additional monitoring coverage
- Coordinating with [[1101_BWARS_Authority_Doctrine|BWARS]] financial security divisions

Limited reserve operations may continue through controlled channels.

---

# PERSONNEL ACTIONS

The owner and management personnel responsible for the facility have been placed under Coalition custody at Tyrants Refuge pending tribunal review.

This action is not a final determination of guilt.

However, the Coalition considers reserve security negligence to be an unacceptable failure of institutional duty.

Charges under review may include:

- Gross negligence
- Failure to maintain protected infrastructure
- Internal misconduct
- Procedural concealment
- Failure to report security vulnerabilities
- Possible conspiracy-related offenses

---

# PUBLIC SAFETY RESPONSE

Residents may notice increased security activity near financial districts, administrative buildings, vault corridors, and reserve transport routes.

Temporary measures may include:

- Expanded patrols
- Entry inspections
- Surveillance upgrades
- Randomized checkpoint activity
- Building access restrictions
- Structural security audits
- Temporary closure of adjacent service areas

These measures are preventative and should not be interpreted as evidence of broader financial instability.

---

# ECONOMIC CONTINUITY

The Coalition [[1300_Treasury_Authority_Charter|Treasury Authority]] confirms that the broader reserve system remains operational.

No public panic, hoarding, unauthorized withdrawals, or rumor-based financial action is warranted.

Residents are reminded that deliberate dissemination of false financial information may be punishable under Coalition economic stability provisions.

---

# REQUEST FOR PUBLIC COOPERATION

Residents are requested to report:

- Suspicious digging
- Unusual late-night movement
- Unauthorized transport of crates or vault materials
- Tampering with surveillance infrastructure
- Unregistered building work
- Unexplained sudden wealth
- Staff misconduct involving financial facilities

All reports may be submitted through approved [[1101_BWARS_Authority_Doctrine|BWARS]] or district administration channels.

---

# CLOSING STATEMENT

The Coalition remains committed to protecting financial stability, reserve integrity, and public confidence.

This incident demonstrates the importance of modern security infrastructure, personnel accountability, and continuous public vigilance.

Security is strongest when institutions and citizens remain equally alert.

---

END OF REPORT
