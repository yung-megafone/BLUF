## TREASURY AUTHORITY CHARTER

### Coalition Economic and Financial Oversight Authority

> [!info] ARCHIVAL STATUS

---

# ARTICLE IX — FOUNDING & PURPOSE

## § 9-1 Establishment of the Treasury

The Treasury Authority is hereby designated as the governing financial body responsible for:

- material valuation,
    
- economic standardization,
    
- reserve oversight,
    
- transaction regulation,
    
- and long-term Coalition financial stability.
    

The Treasury additionally maintains authority over State-recognized conversion standards and Reserve Bank authorization.

---

## § 9-2 Primary Purpose

The Treasury Authority exists to:

- stabilize the Coalition economy,
    
- standardize valuation systems,
    
- maintain trade continuity,
    
- regulate financial institutions,
    
- supervise Reserve Bank operations,
    
- and preserve confidence in State-backed economic systems.
    

---

# ARTICLE X — TREASURY AUTHORITY

## § 10-1 Exclusive Economic Authority

The Treasury maintains sole authority over:

- material-to-currency conversion standards,
    
- State-backed valuation systems,
    
- Reserve Bank licensing,
    
- transaction tax policy,
    
- and economic stabilization directives.
    

Unauthorized establishment of alternative conversion systems constitutes a Coalition economic offense.

---

## § 10-2 Reserve Bank Oversight

Reserve Banks operating within Coalition territory:

- remain subject to Treasury oversight,
    
- must comply with State conversion standards,
    
- and may not impose unauthorized transaction fees or valuation manipulation.
    

The Treasury retains authority to:

- suspend operations,
    
- revoke Reserve authorization,
    
- seize fraudulent assets,
    
- or intervene directly in cases of economic instability.
    

---

# ARTICLE XI — ECONOMIC ENFORCEMENT

## § 11-1 Economic Violations

Manipulation of:

- conversion rates,
    
- reserve valuation systems,
    
- Treasury-backed assets,
    
- or State transaction standards
    

shall constitute Economic Violations under Coalition Penal Doctrine.

---

> [!warning] BWARS ENFORCEMENT NOTICE  
> Violations involving fraudulent valuation systems, counterfeiting, reserve manipulation, or unauthorized taxation may result in asset seizure, imprisonment, or permanent trade prohibition.

---

## § 11-2 Financial Continuity

The Treasury may enact emergency stabilization measures during:

- economic collapse,
    
- reserve shortages,
    
- inflationary instability,
    
- continuity emergencies,
    
- or wartime disruption.
    

Emergency powers may include:

- temporary rate adjustment,
    
- controlled trade restriction,
    
- emergency taxation,
    
- and reserve redistribution.
    

---

# ARTICLE XII — FINAL PROVISION

The Coalition recognizes economic continuity as essential to territorial stability.

The Treasury Authority therefore exists not merely to regulate commerce, but to preserve the operational continuity of the Coalition itself.

---

END OF CHARTER
