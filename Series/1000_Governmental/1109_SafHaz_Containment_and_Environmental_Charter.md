# CHARTER 1109

## SafHaz Containment and Environmental Protection Charter

### Hazardous Materials Containment Standardization

CLASSIFICATION: `L0`  
STATUS: `ACTIVE`  
AUTHORITY: BLUCOR`  
AUTHORIZED BY: `HIGH TABLE

---
# PURPOSE

This charter formally establishes SafHaz as the Coalition authority responsible for hazardous material containment, environmental stabilization, radiological security, strategic contamination oversight, and emergency containment operations within Coalition territory.

SafHaz shall operate as both a regulatory and operational authority under the supervision of BluCor and in coordination with ReCor, BWARS, Treasury Authority, NSIC, and continuity oversight personnel where applicable.

---

# PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES

SafHaz shall maintain authority over:

- Hazardous material storage
    
- Radiological containment operations
    
- Biological hazard response
    
- Chemical containment systems
    
- Environmental remediation
    
- Secure transport supervision
    
- Industrial contamination review
    
- Underground containment infrastructure
    
- Strategic waste management
    
- Emergency containment response
    
- Long-term environmental stabilization
    
- Restricted storage oversight
    

SafHaz additionally maintains operational authority over all designated State hazardous containment facilities.

---

# SAFHAZ RADISAFE FACILITY

The SafHaz RadiSafe Facility located at:

`30 Freedom Dr`

is hereby designated as the Coalition's primary authorized hazardous storage and containment facility.

All hazardous, radiological, biological, and restricted strategic materials exceeding regulatory thresholds shall be stored, processed, transported, or secured in accordance with SafHaz doctrine and containment regulations.

Unauthorized storage of restricted materials outside approved SafHaz facilities shall constitute a Coalition offense subject to enforcement action.

---

# EMERGENCY AUTHORITY

During containment emergencies, radiological incidents, contamination events, structural breaches, or environmental instability scenarios, SafHaz may temporarily assume emergency operational authority over affected zones.

Emergency authority may include:

- evacuation orders,
    
- transport suspension,
    
- infrastructure lockdown,
    
- quarantine designation,
    
- restricted access enforcement,
    
- contamination perimeter establishment,
    
- and emergency demolition authorization.
    

BWARS security units may be requested to assist containment operations where necessary.

---

# INTERAGENCY COORDINATION

SafHaz shall coordinate with:

## ReCor

for:

- infrastructure stability,
    
- underground construction review,
    
- structural remediation,
    
- and containment engineering.
    

## BWARS

for:

- perimeter enforcement,
    
- emergency lockdown,
    
- restricted zone security,
    
- and containment enforcement.
    

## Treasury Authority

for:

- strategic material accounting,
    
- State remediation funding,
    
- and hazardous asset oversight.
    

## NSIC

for:

- classified strategic material coordination,
    
- restricted continuity programs,
    
- and compartmentalized containment operations.
    

---

# CONTAINMENT PHILOSOPHY

SafHaz recognizes that environmental collapse, contamination spread, and infrastructure instability represent existential threats to Coalition continuity.

Containment doctrine therefore prioritizes:

- long-term survivability,
    
- controlled infrastructure growth,
    
- strategic redundancy,
    
- layered containment systems,
    
- and preservation of habitable territory.
    

The Coalition further recognizes that containment failure may at times present a greater threat than direct military confrontation.

---

# RESTRICTED OPERATIONS

Certain SafHaz operations, facilities, transport corridors, underground sectors, and containment programs may remain compartmentalized under higher-level continuity classifications.

Personnel without appropriate authorization may be denied operational awareness where strategic necessity requires.

---

# SYMBOL OF AUTHORITY

SafHaz facilities, personnel, vehicles, and containment infrastructure shall operate under the recognized authority seal of the Coalition Containment and Environmental Authority.

Unauthorized duplication or misuse of SafHaz insignia, credentials, transport markings, or containment authorization codes shall constitute a criminal offense.

---

# FINAL PROVISION

The Coalition recognizes that survival depends not only upon military defense, but upon the ability to safely contain the consequences of its own industrial, scientific, and strategic ambitions.

SafHaz exists to ensure that the dangers created in pursuit of continuity do not ultimately consume the lands themselves.

---
