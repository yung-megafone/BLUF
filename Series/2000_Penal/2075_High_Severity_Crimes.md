# § 75-1 High Treason

## Charge

Acts constituting High Treason include but are not limited to:

### A.

Unprovoked attacks directed against:

- BluCor,
    
- BWARS authorities,
    
- Reserve Bank infrastructure,
    
- Treasury facilities,
    
- or state-recognized entities.
    

### B.

Violations of the BluCor Unified Legislative Framework (`BULF`) resulting in:

- states of emergency,
    
- continuity destabilization,
    
- widespread panic,
    
- or fear of hostile attack.
    

### C.

Deliberate sabotage or destruction involving:

- critical infrastructure,
    
- continuity systems,
    
- strategic facilities,
    
- or state operational assets.
    

### D.

Attempted assassination,  
direct threats,  
or acts intended to undermine:

- sovereignty,
    
- governance,
    
- continuity authority,
    
- or regional stability.
    

### E.

Espionage,  
collaboration with hostile factions,  
or disclosure of strategic information endangering Coalition security.

---

## Punishment

- Permanent incarceration within Tyrant’s Refuge MS until expiration of the guilty.
    
- Confiscation of all personal property, financial assets, and associated holdings.
    
- Permanent revocation of rights involving:
    
    - state property,
        
    - trade,
        
    - governance participation,
        
    - or operational authority.
        
- Treasury penalties proportional to the scale of the threat or resulting damage.
    
- Expanded scrutiny and investigative review involving:
    
    - affiliates,
        
    - organizations,
        
    - contractors,
        
    - and associated personnel  
        under suspicion of aiding treasonous activity.
        

---

# § 75-2 Conspiracy and Facilitation of Criminal Acts

## Charge

Planning,  
organizing,  
assisting,  
financing,  
or facilitating illegal acts directed against:

- BluCor,
    
- BWARS authorities,
    
- Treasury systems,
    
- continuity infrastructure,
    
- or affiliated organizations.
    

This includes:

- logistical support,
    
- operational planning,
    
- covert coordination,
    
- or material assistance.
    

---

## Punishment

- Imprisonment for no less than `15 years`.
    
- Treasury fines equal to the assessed value of damages or losses incurred by the state.
    
- Permanent revocation of rights to:
    
    - conduct business,
        
    - hold governance positions,
        
    - or operate within BluCor-controlled territories.
        
- Confiscation of all assets, facilities, or holdings linked to the conspiracy.
    

---

# § 75-3 Egregious Financial Crimes

## Charge

Violations of:

- contractual obligations,
    
- Treasury agreements,
    
- economic stabilization doctrine,
    
- or financial integrity systems
    

where such violations:

- substantially impact the Coalition economy,
    
- destabilize Treasury operations,
    
- involve systematic fraud,
    
- or constitute organized embezzlement schemes.
    

---

## Punishment

- Imprisonment for no less than `20 years`, with eligibility for life imprisonment depending on severity and economic impact.
    
- Full financial restitution and Treasury penalties equal to or exceeding the total assessed damage.
    
- Confiscation of all:
    
    - fraudulent assets,
        
    - financial instruments,
        
    - businesses,
        
    - or unlawfully obtained holdings.
        
- Permanent disqualification from entering contracts involving:
    
    - BluCor,
        
    - Treasury Authority,
        
    - BWARS entities,
        
    - or affiliated organizations.
        
- Revocation of business licenses and potential dissolution of corporate status.
