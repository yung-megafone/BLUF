# Section 1: Overview

This article establishes minimum clearance and access requirements governing operations within the:

```text
BluCor Strategization Complex (BSC)
```

The provisions herein are classified as:

- Core National Operations (`CNO`) Core Secrets,
    
- Enhanced Controlled Information (`ECI`),
    
- and Special Access Programs (`SAP`).
    

Unauthorized possession or awareness of protected information remains punishable pursuant to continuity security doctrine and Coalition treason statutes.

---

# Section 2: Clearance Levels

Clearance levels governing access within the BSC are structured as follows.

---

# Subsection 2.1 — Clearance Level 1 (Basic Clearance)

## Access

General entry to:

- non-sensitive areas,
    
- logistical corridors,
    
- and public operational sections  
    within the BSC.
    

---

## Requirements

### A.

Completion of:

```text
BluCor Security Training Module A
```

### B.

Successful background investigation completed within the preceding:

```text
12 months
```

---

## Restrictions

Personnel shall possess:

- no access to classified information,
    
- restricted systems,
    
- or compartmentalized operational zones.
    

---

# Subsection 2.2 — Clearance Level 2 (Operational Clearance)

## Access

Includes:

- Level 1 privileges,
    
- designated operational sectors,
    
- and approved logistical infrastructure areas.
    

---

## Requirements

### A.

Successful completion of:

```text
BluCor Security Training Module B
```

### B.

Approval from:

```text
BWARS Operations Office
```

---

## Restrictions

Access remains limited to:

- operational data,
    
- logistical systems,
    
- and approved continuity support functions.
    

No access to strategic doctrine or classified operational intelligence shall be granted.

---

# Subsection 2.3 — Clearance Level 3 (Strategic Clearance)

## Access

Includes:

- Levels 1 and 2 privileges,
    
- strategic planning systems,
    
- and approved intelligence review areas.
    

---

## Requirements

### A.

Completion of:

```text
BluCor Intelligence Analysis Training
```

### B.

Favorable polygraph examination results.

---

## Restrictions

Personnel shall not possess access to:

- classified military operations,
    
- SAP programs,
    
- or compartmentalized strategic intelligence.
    

---

# Subsection 2.4 — Clearance Level 4 (Executive Clearance)

## Access

Includes:

- Levels 1 through 3 privileges,
    
- strategic command sectors,
    
- and entry to the:
    

```text
BWARS WarRoom
```

---

## Requirements

### A.

Certification in:

```text
Advanced Strategic Operations
```

### B.

Nomination by a recognized BluCor faction leader.

### C.

Approval from the:

```text
Strategic Oversight Panel
```

---

## Restrictions

Level 4 personnel retain only limited access to:

- CNO Core Secrets,
    
- ECI materials,
    
- and SAP operational data.
    

---

# Subsection 2.5 — Clearance Level 5 (Core Access)

## Access

Level 5 personnel retain unrestricted access to:

- all BSC facilities,
    
- strategic command systems,
    
- TOP SECRET archives,
    
- continuity infrastructure,
    
- and ECI/SAP materials.
    

---

## Requirements

### A.

Presidential-level or equivalent factional authorization.

### B.

Compliance with Treasury transparency requirements governing strategic operations.

### C.

Mandatory security recertification every:

```text
6 months
```

---

## Restrictions

Personnel remain subject to:

- continuous monitoring,
    
- Oversight Board review,
    
- Intelligence and Compliance auditing,
    
- and continuity behavioral assessment.
    

---

## Membership Clause

Personnel granted Level 5 Clearance are considered:

```text
indefinite members of national security operations
```

Revocation of membership remains punishable under:

- [[2075_High_Severity_Crimes]]
    
- continuity security doctrine
    
- and Coalition treason statutes.
    

---

# Subsection 2.6 — Authorization of Force

Personnel possessing Level 5 Clearance are authorized to utilize:

```text
any force necessary
```

to preserve:

- security,
    
- continuity,
    
- operational secrecy,
    
- and integrity of BluCor facilities.
    

This authority includes:

- preventative measures,
    
- hostile action response,
    
- and neutralization of unauthorized access threats.
    

---

# Subsection 2.7 — Trespassing and Detainment

Unauthorized entry into facilities designated under this article shall result in:

- immediate detainment,
    
- continuity interrogation review,
    
- and strategic security processing.
    

Detained individuals remain subject to:

- [[2181_Enhanced_Interrogation_Guidelines]]
    
- continuity detention doctrine
    
- and further actions determined by security authorities.
    

---

# Section 3: All Other Projects (AOP) Programs

The:

```text
All Other Projects (AOP)
```

program structure encompasses:

- compartmentalized initiatives,
    
- strategic continuity operations,
    
- and classified projects critical to Coalition preservation.
    

---

# Subsection 3.1 — AOP Oversight

AOP programs shall remain supervised exclusively by:

- Level 5 personnel,
    
- continuity authorities,
    
- and designated intelligence officials.
    

Operations shall undergo periodic review by the:

```text
BluCor Intelligence Committee
```

for alignment with continuity objectives.

---

# Subsection 3.2 — Facility Security

AOP-designated facilities remain protected under:

- continuity emergency authority,
    
- strategic infrastructure doctrine,
    
- and restricted access enforcement statutes.
    

Such facilities are considered immune from:

- external interference,
    
- civilian oversight,
    
- and ordinary judicial inspection.
    

---

# Subsection 3.3 — Reporting and Accountability

Classified briefings regarding AOP projects shall be periodically submitted for:

- BWARS continuity oversight,
    
- strategic preservation review,
    
- and intelligence accountability assessment.
    

Unauthorized disclosure of AOP material remains punishable under:

- treason doctrine,
    
- espionage statutes,
    
- and continuity preservation law.
