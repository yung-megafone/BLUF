# § 70-1 Undue Influence and Corruption

## Charge

Individuals, organizations, or governing entities found guilty of using:

- position,
    
- authority,
    
- influence,
    
- or institutional power
    

to:

- corrupt,
    
- coerce,
    
- manipulate,
    
- or pressure
    

others into illegal activity, unlawful conduct, or actions contrary to Coalition law.

---

## Punishment

- Imprisonment for no less than `10 years`, dependent upon the severity of the corruption.
    
- Severe Treasury fines and restitution to affected individuals or entities.
    
- Public judicial trial and formal denouncement.
    
- Permanent disqualification from positions involving:
    
    - governance,
        
    - oversight,
        
    - Treasury authority,
        
    - or strategic administration  
        within BluCor or affiliated organizations.
        

---

# § 70-2 Guilt by Supply

## Charge

Entities, organizations, or individuals found supplying:

- materials,
    
- logistical support,
    
- equipment,
    
- financial backing,
    
- or strategic resources
    

to:

- criminal organizations,
    
- hostile factions,
    
- insurgent groups,
    
- or destabilizing operations.
    

---

## Punishment

- Imprisonment for no less than `10 years` where support was knowingly provided.
    
- Seizure of all resources or financial assets linked to unlawful supply operations.
    
- Treasury fines equal to `300%` of the value of supplied resources.
    
- Permanent revocation of rights to:
    
    - trade,
        
    - conduct commerce,
        
    - or operate businesses  
        within BluCor-controlled territories.
        
- Liability for damages caused through misuse of supplied goods, including restitution to affected parties.
    

---

# § 70-3 Felony Crimes Related to Currency and Trade Violations

## Charge

Acts including but not limited to:

### A.

Fraudulent alteration or unlawful manipulation of:

- material-to-currency exchange rates,
    
- Treasury conversion systems,
    
- or Reserve market valuations.
    

### B.

Unauthorized charging of transaction fees by:

- Reserve Banks,
    
- Treasury institutions,
    
- or affiliated financial entities.
    

### C.

Participation in:

- black-market trade,
    
- illicit exchange systems,
    
- currency manipulation,
    
- or unauthorized economic activity.
    

---

## Punishment

- Up to `10 years` confinement within Tyrant’s Refuge MS.
    
- Seizure of all assets acquired through unlawful activity.
    
- Treasury penalties equal to up to `300%` of the value of disputed assets or transactions.
    
- Suspension of financial operating authority where applicable.
    

---

# § 70-4 Failure to Maintain Secure Detention Facilities

## Charge

Failure to provide reasonably secure detention or holding facilities for individuals accused or convicted of crimes under Coalition law.

Negligence includes but is not limited to:

- inadequate infrastructure,
    
- failure to maintain security systems,
    
- insufficient staffing,
    
- or operational misconduct resulting in escape or harm.
    

---

## Punishment

- Imprisonment for no less than `5 years` for responsible individuals.
    
- Severe Treasury fines proportionate to the level of negligence.
    
- Temporary suspension of business or operational authority for violating organizations.
    
- Mandatory restitution to victims or affected parties harmed due to the failure.
