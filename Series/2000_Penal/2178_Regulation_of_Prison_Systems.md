# § 78-1 INDEF Prisoners

## Definition

`INDEF` prisoners are individuals sentenced to:

```text
indefinite incarceration
```

under continuity detention authority.

---

## Conditions

Authorized conditions of INDEF detention include:

### A.

No food or water provisions shall be supplied, resulting in eventual expiration.

### B.

No:

- external communication,
    
- visitation,
    
- legal correspondence,
    
- or relief measures
    

shall be permitted.

### C.

INDEF detainees shall remain within solitary confinement environments lacking:

- natural light,
    
- environmental variation,
    
- or unrestricted ventilation.
    

---

# § 78-2 Tyrant’s Refuge Maximum Security (MS)

## Facility Overview

`Tyrant’s Refuge MS` shall function as the primary continuity maximum security detention facility.

---

## Structural Design

### A.

The facility consists of:

```text
18 isolated dormitory cells
```

with each cell designated for a single prisoner.

---

### B.

Cell architecture shall eliminate:

- visual communication,
    
- auditory communication,
    
- and unsanctioned interaction
    

between prisoners.

---

### C.

MS prisoners shall not:

- view daylight,
    
- verbally communicate,
    
- or interact with other detainees.
    

---

# § 78-3 Treatment of MS Prisoners

## Mandates

### A.

Meals shall remain strictly rationed to:

```text
one serving every 36 hours
```

unless modified under continuity disciplinary authority.

---

### B.

Prisoners shall remain under continuous monitoring and shall not engage in direct interaction with guards except where operationally necessary.

---

### C.

Failure to comply with MS regulations may result in:

- sensory deprivation,
    
- disciplinary isolation,
    
- restricted sustenance,
    
- or extension of sentence duration.
    

---

# § 78-4 Execution Protocols

## Mandate

Death penalties authorized under continuity judicial authority shall be carried out within:

```text
72 hours of sentencing
```

with no appeal process recognized.

---

## Method

Execution procedures shall:

- occur in isolation,
    
- remain non-public,
    
- and be excluded from ordinary judicial publication systems.
    

Public disclosure of execution details is prohibited unless authorized by the High Court.

---

# § 78-5 Accountability for Prison Staff

## Charge

Failure of prison personnel to:

- uphold detention regulations,
    
- maintain facility order,
    
- preserve continuity doctrine,
    
- or prevent prisoner misconduct.
    

This includes:

- negligence,
    
- unauthorized communication,
    
- procedural violations,
    
- or collusion with detainees.
    

---

## Punishment

- Immediate termination of employment.
    
- Imprisonment for up to `20 years` within Tyrant’s Refuge MS.
    
- Confiscation of operational credentials and continuity access privileges.
    

---

## Collusion Clause

Personnel found collaborating with prisoners may be subjected to:

- identical sentencing standards,
    
- continuity prosecution,
    
- or treason review
    

equivalent to the detainees involved.
