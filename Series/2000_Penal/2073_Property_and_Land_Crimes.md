# ARTICLE 73

## PROPERTY AND LAND CRIMES

### Coalition Territorial and Infrastructure Enforcement Statutes

CLASSIFICATION: `L0  
STATUS: `ACTIVE`  
AUTHORITY: `BLUCOR / BWARS JUDICIAL AUTHORITY`

---

> [!warning] TERRITORIAL ENFORCEMENT NOTICE  
> The following statutes govern:
> 
> - territorial seizure,
>     
> - unlawful occupation,
>     
> - infrastructure destruction,
>     
> - and trespassing upon protected facilities
>     
> 
> under Coalition continuity doctrine.

---

# § 73-1 Illegal Land Appropriation

## Charge

Unauthorized:

- seizure,
    
- occupation,
    
- annexation,
    
- exploitation,
    
- or control
    

of:

- BluCor-controlled territory,
    
- BWARS-recognized land,
    
- protected infrastructure,
    
- or state-controlled resources
    

without lawful authorization, charter recognition, or approved territorial transfer.

---

## Punishment

- Imprisonment for up to `1000 years`.
    
- Immediate confiscation of unlawfully appropriated land or associated property.
    
- Full restitution to the rightful owner, governing authority, or BluCor.
    
- Permanent revocation of territorial or governance privileges where applicable.
    

---

# § 73-2 Destruction or Alteration of Property

## Charge

Willfully:

- destroying,
    
- sabotaging,
    
- modifying,
    
- dismantling,
    
- or unlawfully altering
    

BluCor property, BWARS infrastructure, Treasury facilities, or state-recognized lands without authorization.

This includes:

- environmental damage,
    
- unlawful excavation,
    
- structural tampering,
    
- or continuity infrastructure interference.
    

---

## Punishment

- Imprisonment for up to `10 years`.
    
- Full restitution equal to the assessed value of damaged property.
    
- Treasury fines proportional to:
    
    - environmental impact,
        
    - repair costs,
        
    - continuity disruption,
        
    - or infrastructural destabilization.
        

---

# § 73-3 Trespassing on Secure Areas

## Charge

Unauthorized entry into:

- restricted BluCor territories,
    
- continuity facilities,
    
- BWARS-controlled sectors,
    
- classified operational zones,
    
- or secured infrastructure areas.
    

Trespassing includes:

- bypassing security measures,
    
- unlawful exploration,
    
- unauthorized surveying,
    
- or remaining within restricted zones after notice of removal.
    

---

## Punishment

- Imprisonment for up to `5 years`.
    
- Confiscation of all unauthorized materials or items discovered within secured areas.
    
- Treasury fines or restitution for:
    
    - property damage,
        
    - operational disruption,
        
    - or security compromise resulting from trespass.
        

---
