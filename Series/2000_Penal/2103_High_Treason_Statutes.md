# DIRECTIVE 2103

## HIGH TREASON STATUTES

### Penal and Judicial Series

CLASSIFICATION: `L0  
STATUS: `ACTIVE`

AUTHORIZED BY: `HIGH TABLE`

---

# PURPOSE

This directive establishes offenses constituting High Treason against the [[1102_BluCor_Administration_Charter|BluCor]] Strategic Coalition.

High Treason is defined as intentional conduct threatening continuity, sovereignty, strategic infrastructure, or survival of the Coalition.

---

# SECTION I — TREASONOUS ACTS

High Treason includes:

- Attempted overthrow of Coalition authority
- Strategic sabotage
- Collaboration with hostile factions
- Unauthorized disclosure of classified operations
- Orbital system compromise
- Strategic weapons theft
- Treasury destabilization
- Infrastructure destruction
- Assisting foreign invasion
- Intelligence leakage
- Continuity sabotage

---

# SECTION II — CLASSIFIED TREASON

Disclosure of:

- Continuity plans
- Missile locations
- Orbital systems
- Restricted facilities
- [[1150_WXBRO_Strategic_Oversight|WXBRO]] authority
- [[9007_NOX_ULTIMA|NOX ULTIMA]] references

may constitute Classified Treason.

---

# SECTION III — PRESUMPTION OF HOSTILITY

Actions threatening strategic continuity may be treated as hostile regardless of claimed intent.

Intent does not negate damage.

---

# SECTION IV — SENTENCING

High Treason penalties may include:

- Permanent detainment
- Administrative erasure
- Labor reassignment
- District exile
- Asset seizure
- Tribunal sentencing
- Execution under emergency authority

Public disclosure of sentencing is not guaranteed.

---

# SECTION V — STRATEGIC DOCTRINE

Treason is not disagreement.

Treason is conduct that weakens the state's ability to continue existing.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

A civilization rarely dies from attack alone.  
It dies when its own structure stops protecting itself.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
