# Article N: Founding & Authority

## § 0-1 Establishment of BluCor

### (a)

BluCor is hereby recognized as the founding entity responsible for the creation, operation, and continued development of the Complex, including but not limited to:

- energy research,
    
- infrastructure development,
    
- and continuity-era stabilization systems.
    

---

## § 0-2 Supreme Authority

### (a)

BluCor retains overarching authority to:

- commission facilities,
    
- personnel,
    
- and operations
    

within the Complex.

### (b)

BluCor may establish, restructure, or dissolve subordinate entities at its discretion.

### (c)

BluCor shall allocate resources as necessary to ensure:

- stability,
    
- security,
    
- continuity,
    
- and long-term development of the Complex.
    

---

## § 0-3 Strategic Operations Hub

The BluCor Strategization Complex (`BSC`) shall serve as the central hub for:

- strategic planning,
    
- operational oversight,
    
- continuity governance,
    
- and Coalition administration.
    

---

## § 0-4 Series Authorization

### (a)

BluCor may create legislative series as required to:

- address emerging needs,
    
- establish continuity doctrine,
    
- or provide additional regulatory frameworks.
    

### (b)

All legislative series shall function as extensions of the foundational framework and require BluCor leadership approval prior to enactment.

---

# Article II: The Board of Warfare Alliances & Regional Stability (BWARS)

## § 2-1 Purpose and Structure

### (a)

BWARS shall operate as a stabilizing authority under BluCor supervision focused upon:

- inter-faction cooperation,
    
- regional stability,
    
- and continuity preservation.
    

### (b)

BWARS shall maintain authority regarding:

- land acquisition,
    
- declarations of war,
    
- judicial review,
    
- and conflict resolution procedures.
    

---

## § 2-2 Powers of BWARS

### (a)

Recognized heads of "Cor" factions may petition BWARS for:

#### (i)

Land expansion or reassignment.

#### (ii)

Formal declarations of war against entities threatening state stability.

#### (iii)

Formal charges of treason against entities undermining continuity or regional order.

### (b)

All BWARS decisions remain subject to BluCor leadership approval.

---

## § 2-3 Emergency Authority

### (a)

During regional emergencies, BWARS may expedite operational decisions regarding:

- security,
    
- logistics,
    
- infrastructure,
    
- and continuity operations.
    

### (b)

All emergency decisions shall remain subject to BluCor oversight and review.

---

# Article III: BluCor Conflict Policy

## § 3-1 Wartime Doctrine

### (a)

BluCor shall maintain formal neutrality and provide support to victimized entities during conflict.

### (b)

BluCor retains sole authority to determine victimized status.

---

## § 3-2 Protection of Infrastructure and Personnel

### (a)

Aggression directed toward:

- BluCor infrastructure,
    
- facilities,
    
- personnel,
    
- continuity systems,
    
- or strategic assets
    

shall constitute High Treason.

### (b)

Offenders shall face immediate response under BluCor authority.

### (c)

BWARS may issue judgment in treason proceedings while BluCor retains final authority over sentencing and enforcement.

---

# Article IV: Subordinate Departments

## § 4-1 Commissioning of Departments

### (a)

BluCor may establish or dissolve departments including but not limited to:

#### (i)

Research Divisions

#### (ii)

Defense Sectors

#### (iii)

Energy Development Units

#### (iv)

Infrastructure Management Teams

---

## § 4-2 Autonomy of Departments

### (a)

Subordinate departments may operate with limited autonomy while remaining under BluCor oversight.

### (b)

All departmental directives remain subject to Strategization Complex approval.

---

## § 4-3 Departmental Accountability

### (a)

Departments shall submit routine performance reports with continued authorization dependent upon satisfactory operational review.

---

# Article VI: BlaCor Defense Development

## § 6-1 Founding and Purpose

### (a)

BlaCor shall operate as the official Defense Research and Development Organization under BluCor authority.

### (b)

BlaCor objectives include:

#### (i)

Defense equipment and systems development.

#### (ii)

Engineering and testing of defensive technologies.

#### (iii)

Strategic preparedness through innovation.

### (c)

BlaCor shall report directly to the BluCor Strategization Complex.

---

WhiCor shall operate as the Aerospace Research and Development Organization under BluCor authority.

### (b)

#### (i)

#### (iii)

### (c)

# Article VIII: BroCor Agricultural Charter

## § 8-1 Founding and Purpose

### (a)

BroCor is established as the Agriculture and Livestock Organization responsible for food production and agricultural continuity.

### (b)

BroCor responsibilities include:

#### (i)

#### (ii)
