# BLUCOR STRATEGIC COALITION
## CLASSIFIED DIRECTIVE

DIRECTIVE ID: BSC-2025-002  
INCIDENT: GRCOR TREASURY BREACH  
CLEARANCE LEVEL: 4  
STATUS: ACTIVE INVESTIGATION  
DISTRIBUTION: RESTRICTED  
DATE OF ISSUE: 2025-08-03

---

## INCIDENT SUMMARY

On 2025-08-02, Coalition authorities confirmed a coordinated breach and asset theft operation targeting the GrCor Treasury facility located within SpawnTown Administrative District.

The incident represents one of the largest unauthorized appropriations of Coalition-controlled financial reserves recorded within the current operational cycle.

Initial reports indicate the breach involved premeditated reconnaissance, internal procedural awareness, and deliberate avoidance of standard BWARS patrol visibility.

---

## LOCATION

FACILITY:
GrCor Treasury Repository

REGION:
SpawnTown Central Administrative Sector

FACILITY CLASSIFICATION:
FINANCIAL INFRASTRUCTURE / CONTROLLED ACCESS

---

## KNOWN DAMAGES

Confirmed losses include:

- Strategic reserve materials
- Coalition currency storage assets
- Secured trade commodities
- Classified financial records
- Treasury access credentials
- Encrypted storage ledgers

Additional inventory reconciliation remains ongoing.

Physical structural damage to the Treasury facility was limited, suggesting emphasis on stealth extraction over destructive assault.

---

## PRIMARY FINDINGS

Preliminary NSIC assessment indicates:

- Multiple entry vectors may have been utilized
- Surveillance blind spots were intentionally exploited
- Security rotation timing may have been observed in advance
- Internal procedural knowledge was likely possessed by one or more involved entities

Evidence of tunnel usage beneath the Treasury sector remains under investigation.

---

## PERSONS OF INTEREST

Several entities and affiliated groups are currently classified as Persons of Interest under Coalition investigative authority.

Potential connections to dissident logistical networks and rogue external factions have not been ruled out.

At this time, no formal attribution has been publicly authorized.

---

## BWARS RESPONSE ACTIONS

The following measures are authorized effective immediately:

- Increased BWARS patrol presence within SpawnTown
- Expanded checkpoint operations
- Financial transaction monitoring
- Temporary restriction of select underground transit corridors
- NSIC surveillance escalation
- Tactical audits of all Treasury-adjacent infrastructure

Personnel are reminded that obstruction of Treasury recovery operations constitutes a criminal offense under Coalition Penal Code.

---

## INFORMATION CONTROL

Public disclosure regarding total losses remains restricted.

Personnel are prohibited from discussing:

- Estimated reserve quantities
- Surveillance failures
- Internal suspect lists
- Treasury vault schematics
- NSIC investigative procedures

Unauthorized dissemination of operational details shall be classified as interference with Coalition recovery operations.

---

## STRATEGIC CONCERNS

Coalition leadership recognizes the Treasury breach as both:

- A financial security failure
- A symbolic challenge to Coalition authority

Maintaining public confidence in institutional stability is considered a priority objective.

---

## OPERATIONAL NOTES

NSIC and BWARS personnel are authorized expanded investigative authority within SpawnTown jurisdiction until further notice.

All facilities handling Coalition reserves shall review internal security posture immediately.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
