## PURPOSE

To authorize immediate containment and neutralization procedures following confirmed discovery of unauthorized weapons development activities involving BlaCor personnel and sanctioned OrCor research contacts.

This directive further authorizes execution of Warrant B6M under Coalition continuity authority.

---

## INCIDENT SUMMARY

NSIC surveillance divisions confirmed that personnel operating within BlaCor R&D Facility 001 engaged in unauthorized transfer of refined Yellorium materials to OrCor-associated research entities.

Recovered intelligence indicates transferred materials were intended for development of an experimental nuclear-capable weapons platform.

The activity represents a direct violation of:

- Coalition weapons restrictions
- Strategic materials containment statutes
- Authorized research compartmentalization frameworks
- Article XXV weapons development provisions

Coalition leadership has determined the activity constitutes an existential strategic threat.

## AUTHORIZED RESPONSE

Effective immediately, NSIC Omega units are authorized to execute Warrant B6M.

Approved actions include:

- Total neutralization of on-site personnel
- Seizure of all research materials
- Removal of Yellorium stockpiles
- Destruction of experimental infrastructure
- Elimination of all weapons development evidence
- Controlled structural sabotage operations

No surviving unauthorized personnel are to remain within the operational zone.

- Internal extremist sabotage
- Rogue anti-Coalition actors
- Industrial instability
- Unauthorized reactor experimentation
- Violent dissident infiltration

Direct Coalition involvement shall remain fully deniable.
