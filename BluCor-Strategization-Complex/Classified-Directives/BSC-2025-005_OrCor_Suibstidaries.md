# BLUCOR STRATEGIC COALITION
## CLASSIFIED DIRECTIVE

DIRECTIVE ID: BSC-2025-005  
SUBJECT: ORCOR SUBSIDIARY STABILIZATION FRAMEWORK  
CLEARANCE LEVEL: 6  
STATUS: ACTIVE / DENIABLE  
DISTRIBUTION: HIGH TABLE / NSIC OMEGA / AUTHORIZED WXBRO ONLY  
DATE OF ISSUE: 2025-12-02

---

## PURPOSE

To establish compartmentalized operational procedures regarding the continued utilization of residual OrCor-linked entities following official Coalition dissolution actions under Directive BSC-2025-003.

This directive authorizes restricted strategic engagement with select unofficial subsidiaries, intermediaries, and deniable operational networks determined to provide continued value to Coalition stability objectives.

---

## BACKGROUND

Following formal dissolution of OrCor, multiple surviving operational cells, logistical contacts, research personnel, and financial intermediaries remained active beyond direct Coalition jurisdiction.

Internal assessment concluded that complete elimination of all residual OrCor infrastructure would result in:

- Loss of specialized technological expertise
- Destabilization of controlled opposition networks
- Reduced justification for expanded BWARS authority
- Degradation of strategic threat continuity
- Loss of deniable operational capabilities

As such, limited continuation frameworks were authorized under compartmentalized oversight.

---

## AUTHORIZED SUBSIDIARY STATUS

Select OrCor-derived entities shall be permitted to continue limited operation under indirect supervision through:

- Shell organizations
- Trade intermediaries
- Independent logistics fronts
- Restricted research cells
- Externalized financial conduits

These entities shall possess no publicly acknowledged Coalition affiliation.

Official Coalition policy remains that OrCor was fully dissolved and all principal members executed.

This narrative shall not be contradicted.

---

## CONTROLLED OPPOSITION DOCTRINE

Authorized OrCor-linked groups may conduct controlled hostile actions where such actions are determined to provide strategic governmental utility.

Approved operational outcomes may include:

- Justification for security expansion
- Reinforcement of public dependency on BWARS
- Demonstration of external threats
- Stress-testing of Coalition infrastructure
- Controlled destabilization exercises
- Validation of emergency powers

Operational severity must remain within acceptable continuity thresholds.

Uncontrolled escalation is prohibited.

---

## FUNDING AUTHORIZATION

Coalition authorities may selectively ignore, redirect, or facilitate non-standard revenue acquisition methods utilized by approved OrCor subsidiaries where operational necessity outweighs enforcement value.

Permitted blind-eye activities may include:

- Resource diversion
- Black-market trading
- Controlled theft operations
- Covert asset laundering
- Proxy financial extraction
- Infrastructure skimming

Direct Coalition attribution must remain impossible.

---

## INFORMATION CONTAINMENT

Knowledge of continued OrCor functionality is restricted exclusively to:

- Authorized High Table representatives
- NSIC Omega personnel
- Select WXBRO command staff
- Designated strategic continuity officers

BWARS standard personnel are not authorized access to this directive.

Any unauthorized discovery of ongoing Coalition-OrCor coordination shall be treated as a catastrophic informational breach.

Containment authority is unrestricted.

---

## PUBLIC POSITION

Public-facing Coalition doctrine shall continue to state:

- OrCor was dismantled
- Leadership figures were executed
- Remaining infrastructure was seized
- Coalition authority prevailed completely

Maintenance of perceived victory is considered operationally essential.

---

## STRATEGIC JUSTIFICATION

Coalition continuity depends not solely upon order, but upon the controlled perception of disorder.

A population absent external threat eventually questions internal authority.

Managed instability remains preferable to uncontrolled dissent.

---

## OPERATIONAL NOTES

Personnel are reminded:

The most effective enemy is one partially controlled.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
