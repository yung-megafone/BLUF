# BLUCOR STRATEGIC COALITION
## CLASSIFIED DIRECTIVE

DIRECTIVE ID: BSC-2025-004  
OPERATION: STARFIRE  
CLEARANCE LEVEL: 5  
STATUS: ACTIVE  
DISTRIBUTION: STRICTLY COMPARTMENTALIZED  
DATE OF ISSUE: 2025-11-14

---

## PURPOSE

To authorize the deployment, concealment, operational maintenance, and strategic utilization of Coalition-controlled orbital surveillance and weapons systems under the STARFIRE Initiative.

This directive establishes the operational framework governing SUR-series satellite systems and associated concealment protocols.

---

## PROGRAM DESIGNATIONS

The STARFIRE Initiative currently consists of three authorized orbital systems.

### SUR1
SUR1 is designated as a high-altitude strategic imaging platform responsible for:

- Territorial observation
- Long-range reconnaissance
- Infrastructure mapping
- Threat monitoring
- Tactical operational support

SUR1 visibility to non-cleared personnel shall be attributed to conventional reconnaissance operations.

---

### SUR2
SUR2 is designated as a subterranean analysis and geological penetration platform.

Capabilities include:

- Subsurface structural imaging
- Underground facility detection
- Bunker and tunnel identification
- Mineral and resource surveying
- Deep terrain anomaly mapping

All technical specifications regarding SUR2 operational methodology remain compartmentalized under WXBRO authority.

Personnel lacking Clearance Level 5 shall be informed only that SUR2 performs advanced geological observation.

---

### SUR3 / STARFIRE
SUR3, designated STARFIRE, is classified as a strategic orbital weapons platform.

STARFIRE possesses the capability to deploy a high-yield non-radioactive fusion payload capable of:

- Extreme thermal destruction
- Deep structural penetration
- Neutralization of hardened installations
- Elimination of subterranean infrastructure
- Obsidian-class bunker compromise

Current estimated destructive capacity exceeds 30B² equivalent damage radius under ideal deployment conditions.

The existence of STARFIRE is considered one of the highest-protected Coalition secrets.

---

## DEPLOYMENT AUTHORIZATION

Deployment of STARFIRE requires:

- Dual authorization from designated WXBRO command authorities
- Confirmation of existential or strategic-level threat conditions
- Full operational blackout protocols
- Simultaneous decoy weapons deployment

Authorized decoy systems include:

- Missile launches
- Conventional artillery escalation
- Airborne strike platforms
- Controlled demolition events
- Other plausible strategic distractions

Under no circumstances shall STARFIRE deployment occur without accompanying red-herring operations intended to obscure Coalition orbital weapons capability.

---

## INFORMATION CONTROL

Knowledge of SUR3 technical functionality is restricted to:

- Authorized WXBRO personnel
- Select NSIC command elements
- Approved Coalition High Table representatives

Intentional disclosure of STARFIRE existence, deployment methodology, or technological capability shall constitute High Treason under Coalition Penal Code.

Containment procedures are authorized immediately upon breach detection.

---

## STRATEGIC JUSTIFICATION

The Coalition recognizes strategic superiority through informational imbalance.

Preservation of uncertainty among hostile entities is considered essential to long-term territorial stability and operational deterrence.

---

## OPERATIONAL NOTES

Personnel are reminded:

The most effective weapon is one the enemy cannot prove exists.

END OF DIRECTIVE
