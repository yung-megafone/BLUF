# BLUCOR STRATEGIC COALITION
## CLASSIFIED DIRECTIVE

DIRECTIVE ID: BSC-2026-001  
OPERATION: GRAYWATCH  
CLEARANCE LEVEL: 5  
STATUS: ACTIVE  
DISTRIBUTION: RESTRICTED  
DATE OF ISSUE: 2026-05-23

---

## PURPOSE

To establish standardized intelligence collection, archival, and monitoring capabilities across all Coalition-controlled facilities, districts, strategic assets, and subsidiary installations.

Operation GRAYWATCH authorizes the creation and maintenance of dedicated surveillance, intelligence retention, and information processing sectors within all recognized BSC infrastructure.

---

## DIRECTIVE

Effective immediately, all facilities operating under BSC authority shall designate, construct, or retrofit no fewer than one secured Intelligence Retention Sector (IRS) within the structure.

These sectors shall be responsible for:

- Collection of environmental observations
- Archival of communications intercepts
- Storage of personnel activity logs
- Retention of surveillance recordings
- Monitoring of internal and external operational anomalies
- Preservation of strategic records and classified material

---

## FACILITY REQUIREMENTS

Each Intelligence Retention Sector shall include, where feasible:

- Restricted-access archive room
- Communications monitoring equipment
- Secured storage systems
- Independent backup power
- Hardened access controls
- Direct uplink capability to authorized regional nodes

Facilities designated as CRITICAL INFRASTRUCTURE are authorized expanded monitoring authority under Clearance Level 5 provisions.

---

## AUTHORIZED INSTALLATIONS

This directive applies to, but is not limited to:

- Administrative towers
- Residential complexes
- Industrial sectors
- Transportation infrastructure
- Security installations
- Research facilities
- Mountain and subterranean installations
- Communications relays
- BWARS operational facilities

---

## OPERATIONAL NOTES

Personnel are reminded that intelligence continuity is considered essential to Coalition stability.

Compartmentalization protocols remain in effect.

Unauthorized disclosure of GRAYWATCH infrastructure, procedures, or collected intelligence shall constitute a Level-5 security breach subject to immediate containment action.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
