# BLUCOR STRATEGIC COALITION
## CLASSIFIED DIRECTIVE

DIRECTIVE ID: BSC-2025-003  
SUBJECT: ORCOR SANCTIONS AND DISSOLUTION ORDER  
CLEARANCE LEVEL: 5  
STATUS: ENFORCED  
DISTRIBUTION: INTERNAL SECURITY CHANNELS ONLY  
DATE OF ISSUE: 2025-09-17

---

## PURPOSE

To formally recognize the dissolution of the OrCor Research Collective following confirmed violations of Coalition technological disclosure requirements, operational containment statutes, and classified research reporting mandates.

This directive further establishes sanctions, seizure authorities, and containment procedures regarding all known OrCor personnel, infrastructure, and affiliated assets.

---

## ORGANIZATIONAL SUMMARY

OrCor previously operated as a semi-autonomous Coalition-affiliated research and development entity tasked with:

- Experimental technological development
- Strategic weapons research
- Advanced infrastructure analysis
- Specialized engineering initiatives
- Restricted systems prototyping

Operational autonomy granted to OrCor under prior authorization frameworks has been permanently revoked.

---

## FINDINGS

Coalition audits conducted jointly by NSIC and BWARS investigative divisions determined that OrCor personnel knowingly withheld classified technological discoveries from authorized Coalition oversight authorities.

Confirmed violations include:

- Unauthorized compartmentalization of research
- Concealment of experimental systems
- Failure to disclose strategic technologies
- Improper retention of classified data
- Circumvention of Coalition reporting requirements

Additional findings remain sealed under Clearance Level 5 authority.

---

## DISSOLUTION ORDER

Effective immediately:

- OrCor is hereby dissolved as a recognized Coalition entity
- All OrCor facilities are subject to seizure
- Existing research archives are designated Coalition property
- Remaining technological assets are to be transferred to authorized BSC custody
- Any display of OrCor insignia is prohibited within Coalition territory

Former OrCor personnel are classified as sanctioned individuals pending further review.

---

## ENFORCEMENT ACTIONS

The Coalition authorizes the following:

- Immediate closure of OrCor facilities
- Asset confiscation
- Surveillance escalation on associated personnel
- Communications interception
- Travel restrictions
- Tactical seizure operations where necessary

BWARS and NSIC personnel are granted expanded operational authority regarding OrCor-linked investigations.

---

## DISCIPLINARY OUTCOME

Public records shall indicate that principal OrCor leadership figures were executed under Coalition authority following tribunal proceedings concerning technological concealment and operational betrayal.

All official historical records shall reflect this determination.

Further dissemination of contradictory narratives constitutes historical falsification under Coalition Penal Code.

---

## STRATEGIC CONSIDERATIONS

Coalition leadership recognizes that unrestricted technological compartmentalization poses a direct threat to centralized stability.

No subsidiary organization, regardless of operational value or strategic contribution, shall exist outside authorized Coalition oversight structures.

Maintenance of informational hierarchy remains essential to territorial continuity.

---

## INFORMATION CONTROL

Personnel are prohibited from discussing:

- Specific undisclosed OrCor technologies
- Internal Coalition disagreements regarding sanctions
- Archived OrCor research inventories
- Relocation rumors involving former personnel
- NSIC containment methodologies

All related materials remain compartmentalized.

---

## OPERATIONAL NOTES

Residual OrCor-linked activity may persist in unauthorized territories and abandoned infrastructure sectors.

Coalition personnel are instructed to report all suspected sightings, communications, or recovered materials immediately through NSIC channels.

Personnel are reminded:

Technological control is governmental control.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
