## OFFICIAL STATEMENT

The BluCor Strategic Coalition is pleased to announce the successful completion of multiple residential development initiatives coordinated by ReCor Construction and Infrastructure Authority as part of the Coalition Urban Stability Expansion Program.

The completed projects represent a major step toward strengthening long-term housing availability, workforce accommodation, and regional infrastructure continuity throughout Coalition-controlled territory.

---

## COMPLETED DEVELOPMENTS

The following structures have officially entered service:

### CENTRAL APARTMENT TOWER
A high-density residential tower constructed within the primary administrative district intended to support:

- Registered workers
- Government personnel
- Industrial staff
- Approved long-term residents

The structure includes:

- Multi-level residential sectors
- Controlled access systems
- Utility redundancy infrastructure
- Underground expansion capability
- Integrated surveillance compliance systems

---

### RECOR CIVIC HOTEL
A publicly accessible lodging facility designed to support:

- Authorized travelers
- Infrastructure contractors
- Visiting personnel
- Administrative operations
- Transitional housing needs

The facility operates under Coalition lodging and registration requirements.

All guests remain subject to standard district monitoring procedures.

---

### EASTERN HOUSING COMPLEX
A large-scale residential development established within the eastern district expansion zone intended to support continued population growth and labor distribution requirements.

The housing complex includes:

- Multi-unit residential blocks
- Utility access corridors
- Public distribution access
- Transportation connectivity
- Emergency shelter integration

Additional expansion remains under review pending future infrastructure assessments.

---

## RECOR STATEMENT

ReCor officials stated that the developments were constructed with emphasis on:

- Structural longevity
- Efficient urban density
- Strategic placement
- Future underground expansion compatibility
- Infrastructure continuity standards

Construction oversight remained fully compliant with Coalition architectural and safety doctrine.

## SECURITY AND CIVIC STABILITY

Coalition authorities remind residents that all newly developed residential sectors remain subject to:

- Registration requirements
- Curfew enforcement
- Security inspections
- BWARS patrol jurisdiction
- Infrastructure protection statutes

Surveillance systems have been integrated throughout all public-access areas in accordance with modern Coalition safety standards.

Officials emphasize that secure housing environments contribute directly to public stability and long-term territorial continuity.
