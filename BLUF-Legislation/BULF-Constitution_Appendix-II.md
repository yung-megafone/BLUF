RATIFIED: 2026-05-23

---

# SECTION I — DECLARATION AUTHORITY

The High Table may declare a Continuity Emergency where threats exist involving:

- Insurrection
- Strategic sabotage
- Infrastructure collapse
- Treasury instability
- WMD development
- Orbital threat conditions
- Mass unrest
- Supply failure
- Foreign incursion
- Intelligence compromise

NSIC Omega may issue temporary emergency recommendations pending High Table review.

---

# SECTION II — AUTHORIZED EMERGENCY ACTIONS

During a Continuity Emergency, the Coalition may:

- Suspend movement rights
- Expand surveillance
- Enforce curfews
- Restrict communication
- Freeze financial systems
- Requisition property
- Expand tribunal authority
- Seal districts
- Relocate populations
- Restrict publication
- Deploy BWARS militarized units

---

# SECTION III — CLASSIFIED CONTINUITY OPERATIONS

Emergency continuity operations may remain permanently classified.

Public disclosure is not required where disclosure threatens:

- Stability
- Morale
- Infrastructure security
- Government legitimacy
- Strategic deterrence

---

# SECTION IV — DURATION

Emergency powers shall remain active until the High Table determines continuity has been restored.

No maximum duration is imposed where conditions remain unstable.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

Temporary restrictions become permanent only when instability refuses to end.
