---

# SECTION I — TERRITORIAL AUTHORITY

All territories developed, occupied, excavated, industrialized, fortified, connected, administered, or supplied by Coalition infrastructure since the Foundational Era are recognized as Coalition territory.

Territorial abandonment does not constitute loss of claim.

---

# SECTION II — UNDERGROUND AUTHORITY

Coalition jurisdiction extends vertically beneath all recognized territory.

This includes:

- Tunnels
- Deep storage
- Utility corridors
- Subterranean rail
- Vault systems
- Hidden infrastructure
- Continuity bunkers
- Excavated facilities

Unauthorized underground expansion constitutes encroachment upon Coalition authority.

---

# SECTION III — AIRSPACE AND ORBITAL CLAIMS

The Coalition maintains exclusive authority over:

- Controlled airspace
- Communications towers
- Satellite uplinks
- Orbital systems
- Strategic launch infrastructure

Unauthorized aerial or orbital development is prohibited.

---

# SECTION IV — STRATEGIC CITY DESIGNATION

The following locations may receive Strategic City designation:

- SpawnTown
- RedStone City
- Tyrants Refuge
- Designated industrial sectors
- Administrative arcologies
- Orbital uplink districts

Strategic Cities may receive expanded surveillance, security, and infrastructure authority.

---

# SECTION V — CONTINUITY INFRASTRUCTURE

The Coalition reserves unrestricted authority over infrastructure deemed essential to continuity.

Continuity infrastructure may include:

- Power stations
- Treasury vaults
- Surveillance centers
- Deep archives
- Rail systems
- Data repositories
- Orbital control facilities
- Underground command sectors

Maps and layouts of continuity infrastructure may remain permanently classified.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

Territory is not defined by borders alone.  
Territory is defined by infrastructure capable of enforcing authority.
