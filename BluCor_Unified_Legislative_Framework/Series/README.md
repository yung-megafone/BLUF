# OVERVIEW

The BLUF Archive serves as the centralized legal, administrative, intelligence, infrastructure, continuity, and warfare doctrine repository of the BluCor Strategic Coalition.

The archive preserves constitutional authority, territorial legitimacy, strategic doctrine, public communications, continuity procedures, and operational governance records across all recognized Coalition sectors.

The BLUF system was initially consolidated circa 2024 following increasing territorial expansion, infrastructure complexity, classified operational growth, and continuity concerns.

The modern directive structure was formally standardized following constitutional ratification on 2026-05-23.

---

# PURPOSE OF THE ARCHIVE

The archive exists to preserve:

* Constitutional continuity
* Administrative authority
* Territorial sovereignty
* Infrastructure doctrine
* Strategic warfare policy
* Classified operational records
* Public communications
* Penal enforcement structure
* Historical continuity records
* Emergency governance procedures

The archive ensures that Coalition authority may survive infrastructure collapse, leadership transition, territorial instability, continuity emergencies, or strategic conflict.
