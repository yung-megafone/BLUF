# PURPOSE

NOX ULTIMA establishes final-contingency doctrine for circumstances in which the BluCor Strategic Coalition faces irreversible destruction, hostile occupation, strategic collapse, or loss of sovereign continuity.

The directive exists to ensure that no hostile power, rogue faction, invader, successor regime, or internal usurper inherits Coalition territory, infrastructure, weapons systems, reserves, archives, or strategic capabilities intact.

---

# SECTION I — ACTIVATION CONDITIONS

NOX ULTIMA may be considered only under conditions including:

- Imminent collapse of central Coalition authority
- Total breach of strategic command infrastructure
- Irreversible hostile occupation
- Loss of High Table continuity
- Capture of orbital command systems
- Compromise of strategic missile networks
- Catastrophic betrayal by internal command elements
- Confirmed inability to preserve territorial sovereignty

Activation requires highest available continuity authorization.

Where normal command chains no longer exist, sealed succession protocols apply.

---

# SECTION II — STRATEGIC PRINCIPLE

The Coalition recognizes survival as the highest objective.

Where survival becomes impossible, denial becomes the final expression of sovereignty.

No hostile entity shall inherit a functioning Coalition.

No invader shall inherit the capital.
No traitor shall inherit the archives.
No faction shall inherit the missiles.
No successor shall inherit the machine.

---

# SECTION III — DENIAL NETWORK

NOX ULTIMA authorizes the maintenance of dormant denial infrastructure including:

- Hidden missile sites
- Buried launch facilities
- Sealed command nodes
- Dead infrastructure triggers
- Strategic demolition charges
- Archive destruction systems
- Treasury denial systems
- Underground collapse points
- Orbital denial linkages
- Borderland retaliation sites

The existence, location, and readiness state of these systems shall remain compartmentalized.

---

# SECTION IV — INFRASTRUCTURE DENIAL

Upon activation, priority denial targets may include:

- Command towers
- Treasury vaults
- Missile sites
- Orbital uplink systems
- Strategic archives
- BWARS headquarters
- NSIC facilities
- Power plants
- Rail networks
- Underground corridors
- Hazard containment sites
- Strategic residential deep levels

Infrastructure denial shall be sequenced to prevent hostile recovery.

---

# SECTION V — STRATEGIC RETALIATION

Where hostile destruction of the Coalition is confirmed, retaliation options may include strategic strikes against:

- Invading command centers
- Occupation staging zones
- Captured Coalition infrastructure
- Rogue faction capitals
- Enemy industrial systems
- Strategic resource reserves
- Borderland infiltration routes

Retaliation shall be framed internally as denial of hostile continuity, not vengeance.

---

# SECTION VI — INFORMATION ERASURE

Upon activation, authorized personnel may initiate:

- Archive burn protocols
- Ledger corruption
- Map destruction
- Authentication key destruction
- Classified directive sealing
- Personnel registry erasure
- False continuity trail deployment

A destroyed record is preferable to a captured one.

---

# SECTION VII — SUCCESSION FAILURE

If command succession fails, remaining authorized continuity cells may operate under independent sealed mandate.

Cells shall not attempt public restoration unless strategic conditions permit meaningful sovereignty.

If restoration is impossible, denial remains authorized.

---

# SECTION VIII — PUBLIC NONEXISTENCE

NOX ULTIMA shall not be acknowledged in:

- Public law
- Tribunal records
- Standard military doctrine
- BWARS training materials
- Civil defense plans
- Public statements
- Constitutional appendices

Any discovered reference shall be reclassified, removed, or falsified.

---

# SECTION IX — STRATEGIC JUSTIFICATION

The Coalition was built to survive.

But if survival is denied, inheritance shall also be denied.

The world may continue after the Coalition.

It shall not continue by feeding on its remains.

---

# FINAL DOCTRINAL STATEMENT

Continuity preserved is victory.

Continuity denied to the enemy is also victory.

If the Coalition falls, the lights go out with it.
