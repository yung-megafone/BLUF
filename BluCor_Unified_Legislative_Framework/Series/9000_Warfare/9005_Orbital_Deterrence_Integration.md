# BLUCOR STRATEGIC COALITION
## STRATEGIC DIRECTIVE
### WARFARE DOCTRINE SERIES

DIRECTIVE ID: 9005  
SUBJECT: ORBITAL DETERRENCE INTEGRATION  
CLEARANCE LEVEL: 4  
STATUS: ACTIVE / COMPARTMENTALIZED  
DISTRIBUTION: HIGH TABLE / WXBRO / NSIC STRATEGIC CELLS / AUTHORIZED COMMAND ONLY  
DATE OF ISSUE: 2026-02-03

---

# PURPOSE

This directive establishes doctrine for integrating Coalition orbital systems into strategic deterrence, surveillance, border security, infrastructure protection, and continuity operations.

Orbital systems shall be treated as national-level strategic assets.

---

# SECTION I — ORBITAL ASSET CLASSES

Coalition orbital systems may include:

- Imaging platforms
- Subterranean observation platforms
- Communications relays
- Strategic warning systems
- Deterrence-linked systems
- Classified payload platforms

Public identification of system purpose may differ from operational reality.

---

# SECTION II — SURVEILLANCE INTEGRATION

Orbital observation may support:

- Borderland monitoring
- Missile range security
- Strategic facility inspection
- Unauthorized excavation detection
- Infrastructure damage assessment
- Hostile movement tracking
- Continuity event review

Orbital data may be fused with NSIC and BWARS reporting.

---

# SECTION III — DETERRENCE FUNCTION

Orbital assets serve deterrence through:

- Observation superiority
- Strategic ambiguity
- Rapid damage assessment
- Command continuity
- Enemy movement denial
- Retaliatory credibility

The Coalition shall preserve uncertainty regarding orbital capability.

---

# SECTION IV — STARFIRE COMPARTMENT

References to orbital weapons systems remain compartmentalized.

Personnel cleared under this directive are not automatically cleared into STARFIRE operational details.

Need-to-know restrictions remain absolute.

---

# SECTION V — COMMAND AUTHORITY

Orbital deterrence actions require:

- Valid command authentication
- Threat classification
- Continuity assessment
- WXBRO or High Table approval where applicable
- NSIC verification where intelligence data is involved

Unauthorized tasking of orbital assets constitutes a classified systems breach.

---

# SECTION VI — PUBLIC CONCEALMENT

Public-facing explanations for orbital activity may include:

- Weather observation
- Mapping
- Communications maintenance
- Border survey
- Geological study
- Infrastructure planning

The true scope of orbital deterrence shall not be publicly disclosed.

---

# SECTION VII — STRATEGIC JUSTIFICATION

A state that can see from above is harder to surprise.

A state that cannot be surprised is harder to destroy.

Orbital superiority preserves territorial continuity before conflict begins.

---

Personnel are reminded:

The sky is not empty.  
It is occupied territory.

END OF DIRECTIVE
