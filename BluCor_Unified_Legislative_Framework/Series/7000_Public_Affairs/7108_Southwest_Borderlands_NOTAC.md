# OFFICIAL NOTICE

The BluCor Strategic Coalition hereby issues formal notice regarding the designation of an active missile and bomb testing range within the Southwest Borderlands sector.

The Southwest Borderlands are recognized as the southwestern territorial edge of the Coalition and are subject to enhanced security control, restricted movement, and strategic weapons testing procedures.

---

# RESTRICTED AREA

The designated test range includes obsidian-marked perimeter zones, reinforced testing surfaces, launch areas, impact regions, observation infrastructure, and associated security corridors.

A mandatory exclusion radius of 100 meters from the outermost obsidian edge is in effect.

No person, faction, partner, visitor, contractor, civilian, coalition member, or unaffiliated entity may enter this zone without explicit authorization.

Coordinates will be distributed through authorized channels.

---

# PROHIBITED ACTIVITY

The following are strictly prohibited within or near the exclusion zone:

- Entry
- Attempted entry
- Trespassing
- Loitering
- Photography
- Videography
- Aerial imagery
- Mapping
- Surveying
- Drone observation
- Material collection
- Tunneling
- Excavation
- Unauthorized signal transmission

Attempts to observe or document testing activity may be classified as hostile reconnaissance.

---

# ENFORCEMENT WARNING

The Southwest Borderlands test range is an active weapons testing region.

Violators may face:

- Immediate detention
- Removal from the area
- Tribunal referral
- Confiscation of equipment
- Classification as hostile actors
- Lethal force where deemed necessary

Warning procedures are not guaranteed.

---

# SAFETY HAZARDS

Hazards may include:

- Missile launches
- Bomb testing
- Shockwave effects
- Thermal discharge
- Falling debris
- Structural fragmentation
- Crater formation
- Unexploded ordnance
- Subsurface instability

The Coalition cannot guarantee survival inside restricted proximity.

---

# INFORMATION CONTROL

The public is prohibited from distributing imagery, coordinates, test descriptions, launch observations, payload speculation, or infrastructure details regarding the range.

Such dissemination may violate security law.

---

# CLOSING NOTICE

The Southwest Borderlands are not abandoned lands.

They are controlled, monitored, and dangerous.

Do not approach.

---
