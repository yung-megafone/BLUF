# OFFICIAL ADVISORY

The BluCor Strategic Coalition issues this Public Health and Safety Advisory to remind residents of standard procedures regarding hazardous materials, restricted zones, emergency reporting, sanitation, and community safety.

Public health is a continuity requirement.

---

# HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

Residents must not touch, collect, transport, store, sell, or conceal unknown materials.

This includes:

- Glowing substances
- Sealed containers
- Laboratory debris
- Industrial waste
- Reactor byproducts
- Chemical residues
- Unmarked barrels
- Biological samples
- Contaminated tools

All suspicious materials should be reported immediately.

---

# RESTRICTED FACILITIES

Residents are prohibited from entering:

- Damaged industrial sites
- Sealed research buildings
- Hazard storage areas
- Underground service corridors
- Missile range zones
- Vault-adjacent tunnels
- Quarantine areas
- BWARS closure zones

Restricted areas are restricted because public safety cannot be guaranteed inside them.

---

# SANITATION AND WATER SAFETY

Residents should report:

- Discolored water
- Strange odors
- Dead vegetation near water sources
- Unmarked dumping
- Chemical leakage
- Sewage overflow
- Contaminated storage areas

Do not consume water from unapproved sources during advisory conditions.

---

# BIOLOGICAL SAFETY

Residents should report unusual biological activity including:

- Unknown fungal growth
- Diseased livestock
- Unusual plant decay
- Unidentified samples
- Unregistered medical activity
- Unauthorized laboratory operations

Do not move biological material across districts.

---

# EMERGENCY REPORTING

Report emergencies through authorized channels.

Reports should include:

- Location
- Visible hazard
- Number of affected persons
- Description of material or incident
- Direction of spread if applicable
- Whether restricted infrastructure is nearby

False reports may result in enforcement action.

---

# PUBLIC MOVEMENT DURING INCIDENTS

During safety incidents, residents may be instructed to:

- Shelter in place
- Evacuate
- Avoid specific roads
- Remain indoors
- Close windows
- Report for screening
- Submit to inspection
- Relocate temporarily

Compliance is mandatory.

---

# RUMOR CONTROL

Residents are reminded that unverified health, hazard, or contamination rumors may create panic and obstruct emergency response.

Do not circulate claims unless confirmed by Coalition authorities.

---

# CLOSING STATEMENT

The Coalition protects public health through containment, inspection, and decisive response.

Safety requires discipline.

Discipline requires cooperation.

---

END OF ADVISORY
