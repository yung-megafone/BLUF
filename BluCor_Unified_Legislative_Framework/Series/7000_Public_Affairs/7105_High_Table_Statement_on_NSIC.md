# OFFICIAL STATEMENT

The High Table issues this statement to clarify the role of the National Strategic Intelligence Command (NSIC) within the broader Coalition security and continuity framework.

NSIC remains a lawful, recognized, and necessary institution of the BluCor Strategic Coalition.

Its existence reflects the Coalition’s responsibility to anticipate threats before they become public emergencies.

---

# ROLE OF NSIC

NSIC supports Coalition continuity through:

- Intelligence analysis
- Strategic threat review
- Counter-sabotage support
- Infrastructure protection
- Security coordination
- Restricted investigation
- Emergency advisory functions

Certain NSIC activities are not discussed publicly due to the sensitivity of ongoing threats and the need to protect operational effectiveness.

---

# PUBLIC CONCERNS

The High Table acknowledges that institutions operating partially outside public visibility may create speculation.

However, public visibility is not the same as lawful authority.

NSIC operates under Coalition mandate, constitutional continuity doctrine, and internal review structures.

The absence of public detail should not be mistaken for absence of oversight.

---

# SECURITY NECESSITY

Modern threats do not always announce themselves openly.

Some threats develop through:

- Hidden networks
- Financial manipulation
- Unauthorized excavation
- Information leaks
- Facility compromise
- Foreign-aligned influence
- Restricted technology misuse

NSIC exists to detect and contain such threats before public harm occurs.

---

# PUBLIC COOPERATION

Residents may occasionally encounter NSIC-related restrictions, sealed areas, or information limitations.

Residents are expected to comply with lawful instructions issued by authorized Coalition personnel.

Questions may be submitted through district administration, though certain answers may remain restricted.

---

# LIMITS OF DISCLOSURE

The High Table will not disclose:

- NSIC personnel rosters
- Operational methods
- Facility locations
- Surveillance capabilities
- Classified investigations
- Strategic threat assessments

These limitations exist to protect the public and preserve Coalition continuity.

---

# CLOSING STATEMENT

Security is not always visible.

The public sees the peace.

The institutions preserving that peace often remain unseen.

The High Table reaffirms its confidence in NSIC as an essential component of Coalition stability.

---

END OF STATEMENT
