# PUBLIC NOTICE 7104

## POI1 FACILITY TAKEOVER

### Public Affairs Release

CLASSIFICATION: `L0`  
STATUS: `PUBLIC RELEASE`  
AUTHORITY: `BLUCOR`  
AUTHORIZED BY: `PUBLIC AFFAIRS BUREAU`

---

# OFFICIAL STATEMENT

The [[1102_BluCor_Administration_Charter|BluCor]] Strategic Coalition confirms that a restricted facility identified in public records as POI-1 was temporarily occupied by unauthorized personnel during a localized security incident.

Coalition response units regained control of the facility following containment operations.

The surrounding population is not believed to be under current threat.

---

# INCIDENT SUMMARY

During routine review, authorities identified abnormal activity at POI-1, including unauthorized access, irregular movement, and communication anomalies.

Subsequent response confirmed that unauthorized individuals had entered or occupied portions of the facility.

[[1101_BWARS_Authority_Doctrine|BWARS]] and authorized Coalition personnel established a security perimeter and conducted recovery operations.

---

# PUBLIC ACCESS RESTRICTION

POI-1 and surrounding access routes remain closed to the public pending:

- Structural inspection
- Evidence recovery
- Security review
- Hazard screening
- Access-control restoration
- Personnel accountability review

Unauthorized entry into the area remains prohibited.

---

# PUBLIC SAFETY POSITION

Residents near the area may observe:

- Patrol activity
- Road restrictions
- Lighting operations
- Structural repairs
- Transport vehicles
- Temporary barriers
- Survey teams

These measures are precautionary.

No evacuation is required unless directed by Coalition authorities.

---

# INVESTIGATIVE STATUS

The identities, affiliations, and motives of the unauthorized personnel remain under investigation.

The Coalition is reviewing possible connections to:

- Criminal trespass
- Infrastructure sabotage
- Black-market activity
- Restricted material interest
- Anti-Coalition propaganda
- Unauthorized reconnaissance

No final public attribution has been authorized.

---

# INFORMATION CONTROL

Residents are instructed not to circulate:

- Rumors regarding casualties
- Unverified affiliation claims
- Facility diagrams
- Photographs of response personnel
- Imagery of access points
- Speculation regarding facility purpose

Unverified claims may interfere with investigative integrity and public safety.

---

# COMMUNITY COOPERATION

Residents with relevant information should report:

- Suspicious visitors
- Unauthorized vehicles
- Late-night movement
- Unusual construction noises
- Attempts to purchase maps
- Requests for restricted access information

Reports may be submitted through [[1101_BWARS_Authority_Doctrine|BWARS]] or district administration channels.

---

# CLOSING STATEMENT

The Coalition remains capable of identifying, isolating, and resolving threats to restricted infrastructure.

Protected facilities are protected for public safety.

The perimeter exists because the risk is real.

---

END OF REPORT
