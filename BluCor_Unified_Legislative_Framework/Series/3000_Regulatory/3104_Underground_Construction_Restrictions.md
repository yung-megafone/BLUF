# DIRECTIVE 3104

## UNDERGROUND CONSTRUCTION RESTRICTIONS

### Regulatory Compliance Series

CLASSIFICATION: `L1`  
STATUS: `ACTIVE`  
AUTHORITY: `BLUCOR / RECOR`  
AUTHORIZED BY: `HIGH TABLE`

---

# PURPOSE

This directive establishes regulations governing subterranean excavation, underground expansion, tunnel construction, and deep infrastructure development.

---

# SECTION I — AUTHORIZED EXCAVATION

Underground construction requires:

- [[1104_ReCor_Infrastructure_Charter|ReCor]] approval
- Structural review
- Hazard review
- Infrastructure mapping
- Tunnel registration

---

# SECTION II — RESTRICTED ACTIVITIES

Unauthorized underground activities include:

- Hidden tunnel creation
- Vault approach excavation
- Underground bypass routes
- Unregistered bunker construction
- Concealed chambers
- Strategic facility tunneling

---

# SECTION III — DEPTH RESTRICTIONS

Deep-level excavation beneath strategic districts may require:

- [[1101_BWARS_Authority_Doctrine|BWARS]] review
- [[1102_NSIC_Operational_Authority|NSIC]] review
- Hazard containment approval
- Continuity assessment

---

# SECTION IV — INSPECTION AUTHORITY

Authorized inspectors may:

- Survey tunnels
- Review structural integrity
- Seal unsafe sectors
- Confiscate excavation equipment
- Restrict further expansion

---

# SECTION V — EMERGENCY DENIAL

Underground sectors threatening continuity may be:

- Flooded
- Collapsed
- Sealed
- Demolished
- Requisitioned

---

# SECTION VI — STRATEGIC DOCTRINE

Underground space is strategic space.

Unregulated depth becomes unregulated authority.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

Every tunnel connects two locations.  
The Coalition intends to know both of them.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
