# DIRECTIVE 3114

## BLUCOR REACTOR AUTHORIZATION APPROVAL

### Approval of LiCor Onsite Yellorium Reactor Proposal

CLASSIFICATION: `L1 / INTERNAL`  
STATUS: `APPROVED`

---

# SECTION I — APPROVAL

BluCor formally approves:

```text
Request 3113
```

submitted by LiCor and SafHaz for construction and operation of an onsite Yellorium reactor within the Freedom Dr continuity complex.

Authorization is granted pursuant to:

- continuity infrastructure doctrine,
    
- strategic survivability requirements,
    
- and radiological containment objectives.
    

---

# SECTION II — REACTOR AUTHORIZATION

LiCor is authorized to:

- construct,
    
- fuel,
    
- maintain,
    
- and operate
    

a hardened Yellorium reactor for continuity power generation.

The reactor shall exclusively support:

- radiological protection systems,
    
- continuity ventilation,
    
- emergency communications,
    
- sensor infrastructure,
    
- and facility survivability systems.
    

---

# SECTION III — CONTAINMENT REQUIREMENTS

## Reactor Isolation

The reactor chamber must:

- remain physically isolated from the radiological vault,
    
- maintain independent sealing capability,
    
- and support emergency compartment shutdown procedures.
    

---

## Distance Requirements

The reactor room shall be positioned:

```text
a reasonable strategic distance from the radiological vault
```

to minimize:

- catastrophic chain-risk,
    
- containment compromise,
    
- and simultaneous infrastructure failure.
    

---

## Mandatory Structural Requirements

The reactor chamber shall include:

- reinforced ducrete shielding,
    
- Compound Mesh Barrier reinforcement,
    
- pressure-rated containment barriers,
    
- emergency SCRAM systems,
    
- and automated atmospheric isolation systems.
    

---

# SECTION IV — TUNNEL & FACILITY PROTECTION

No direct unsealed pathway may exist between:

- the reactor chamber,
    
- the radiological vault,
    
- or continuity habitation areas.
    

All interconnecting corridors must support:

- automated blast-door closure,
    
- radiological lockdown,
    
- independent atmospheric isolation,
    
- and manual override capability.
    

---

# SECTION V — FUEL STORAGE & CONTINUITY

LiCor is authorized to maintain:

- secured Yellorium reserves,
    
- processed Cyanite storage,
    
- and continuity fuel stockpiles onsite.
    

Storage facilities must:

- remain hardened,
    
- sealed,
    
- and independently compartmentalized from reactor operations.
    

---

# SECTION VI — OVERSIGHT

SafHaz shall maintain:

- continuous radiation monitoring,
    
- containment verification,
    
- and emergency response readiness.
    

BlaCor retains authority to:

- inspect reactor integrity,
    
- review shielding compliance,
    
- and suspend operations in the event of unacceptable risk conditions.
    

---

# FINAL DIRECTIVE

BluCor formally concludes:

```text
Continuity infrastructure requires survivable independent power generation protected against contamination, sabotage, and environmental failure.
```

Authorization is hereby granted for:

- reactor construction,
    
- continuity integration,
    
- and operational commissioning review.
    

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
