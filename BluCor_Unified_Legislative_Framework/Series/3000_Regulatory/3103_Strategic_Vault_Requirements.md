# DIRECTIVE 3103

## STRATEGIC VAULT REQUIREMENTS

### Regulatory Compliance Series

CLASSIFICATION: `L1`  
STATUS: `ACTIVE`  
AUTHORITY: `BLUCOR / RECOR`  
AUTHORIZED BY: `HIGH TABLE`

---

# PURPOSE

This directive establishes mandatory construction, surveillance, access control, and structural reinforcement standards for strategic vault infrastructure.

---

# SECTION I — DESIGNATED VAULT STATUS

Strategic vault designation applies to facilities storing:

- Currency reserves
- Strategic materials
- Classified records
- Continuity archives
- Weapons systems
- Treasury assets
- Authentication materials

---

# SECTION II — STRUCTURAL REQUIREMENTS

Strategic vaults shall maintain:

- Reinforced construction
- Obsidian hardening
- Underground isolation
- Layered blast protection
- Redundant structural supports
- Independent ventilation
- Emergency lockdown systems

---

# SECTION III — ACCESS CONTROL

Vault access requires:

- Multi-stage authorization
- Access logging
- Surveillance monitoring
- Identity verification
- Restricted scheduling

Emergency override authority remains classified.

---

# SECTION IV — TUNNEL PROTECTION

Vault facilities shall maintain underground intrusion monitoring including:

- Seismic detection
- Structural review
- Tunnel inspection
- Restricted excavation enforcement

Unauthorized underground approach constitutes hostile activity.

---

# SECTION V — STRATEGIC DOCTRINE

A reserve survives longest when its location, structure, and access remain uncertain.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

The strongest vault is the one the attacker cannot fully map.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
