# REQUEST 3113

## ONSITE YELLORIUM REACTOR AUTHORIZATION REQUEST

### Freedom Dr Continuity Energy Proposal

CLASSIFICATION: `L1 / INTERNAL`  
STATUS: `PENDING REVIEW`

---

# SECTION I — PURPOSE

LiCor formally requests authorization from BluCor and associated energy authorities for construction and operation of an onsite Yellorium reactor at the Freedom Dr continuity infrastructure complex.

The reactor shall:

- provide independent continuity power,
    
- eliminate dependence on external power corridors,
    
- and reduce contamination exposure risk caused by external maintenance access.
    

---

# SECTION II — JUSTIFICATION

LiCor and SafHaz assess that:

- external infrastructure access increases contamination vulnerability,
    
- tunnel exposure events may compromise continuity survivability,
    
- and independent onsite generation is necessary for long-duration sealed operations.
    

The proposed reactor is intended exclusively for:

- continuity infrastructure,
    
- radiological protection systems,
    
- ventilation,
    
- monitoring systems,
    
- and emergency communications.
    

---

# SECTION III — FUEL EXCHANGE AGREEMENT

LiCor formally agrees to:

- provide used Cyanite fuel material,
    
- maintain secure radiological accounting,
    
- and participate in authorized material exchange programs.
    

---

## Exchange Commitment

LiCor proposes:

```text
1:1 or equivalent-value Cyanite-to-Yellorium exchange agreements
```

subject to Treasury valuation standards and BluCor approval.

---

# SECTION IV — CONTINUITY RESERVE STORAGE

LiCor shall implement:

- secure radiological reserve storage,
    
- hardened fuel containment,
    
- and continuity fuel reserves onsite.
    

Reserve storage is intended to:

- maintain operational survivability during shortages,
    
- prevent emergency shutdown conditions,
    
- and ensure uninterrupted radiological containment capability.
    

---

# SECTION V — SECURITY & SAFETY

The proposed reactor facility shall include:

- reinforced ducrete shielding,
    
- Compound Mesh Barrier reinforcement,
    
- emergency SCRAM systems,
    
- automated radiation monitoring,
    
- and compartmentalized containment barriers.
    

SafHaz shall maintain:

- continuous sensor oversight,
    
- contamination monitoring,
    
- and emergency response readiness.
    

---

# FINAL REQUEST

LiCor formally concludes:

```text
Continuity infrastructure cannot remain dependent upon vulnerable external energy pathways.
```

Authorization is respectfully requested for:

- reactor construction,
    
- fuel allocation,
    
- and continuity certification review.
    

---

END OF REQUEST
