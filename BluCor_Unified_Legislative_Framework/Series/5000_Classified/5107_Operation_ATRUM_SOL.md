# DIRECTIVE 5107

## OPERATION ATRUM SOL

### Classified Operations Series

CLASSIFICATION: `L4`  
STATUS: `ACTIVE`  
AUTHORITY: `BLUCOR / NSIC`  
AUTHORIZED BY: `HIGH TABLE`

---

# PURPOSE

Operation ATRUM SOL authorizes the establishment, maintenance, concealment, and compartmentalized operation of a fabricated industrial and research facility intended for the covert development, testing, refinement, containment, and deployment study of strategic weapon systems.

The existence, purpose, operational capability, staffing structure, and logistical support network associated with ATRUM SOL shall remain compartmentalized under Continuity Black doctrine.

The designated installation shall outwardly operate under a civilian or industrial pretext determined by BluCor administrative leadership and NSIC strategic continuity personnel.

---

# AUTHORIZED RESEARCH

The facility is authorized to conduct research involving:

- Strategic missile systems
    
- Radiological materials
    
- Directed energy experimentation
    
- High-yield explosive systems
    
- Strategic bunker penetration systems
    
- Orbital weapons integration
    
- Advanced ignition mechanisms
    
- Yield containment analysis
    
- Fallout containment and dispersal modeling
    
- Experimental energy generation systems
    
- Weaponized material refinement
    

Additional projects may be authorized under emergency continuity powers.

---

# FACILITY CONCEALMENT

The installation shall be concealed through layered operational deception including:

- False industrial classification
    
- Restricted cartographic disclosure
    
- BWARS perimeter restriction doctrine
    
- Fabricated logistics records
    
- Segmented personnel routing
    
- Underground compartmentalization
    
- Isolated infrastructure corridors
    
- Controlled aerial visibility
    
- Public-facing infrastructure masking
    

Public explanation for the site may include:

- Mining operations
    
- Industrial waste processing
    
- Transit infrastructure
    
- Geological surveying
    
- Energy refinement
    
- Strategic reserve warehousing
    

No public-facing records shall reference weapons development activity.

---

# PERSONNEL RESTRICTIONS

Only authorized NSIC and BluCor continuity-cleared personnel may access the primary operational sectors of ATRUM SOL.

Personnel assigned to logistical, engineering, or construction support may be intentionally compartmentalized and denied operational awareness.

Unauthorized disclosure of operational information shall constitute High Treason under Coalition Penal Doctrine.

---

# STRATEGIC JUSTIFICATION

The Coalition recognizes that continuity cannot depend solely upon visible deterrence systems.

Operation ATRUM SOL exists to ensure:

- Strategic survivability
    
- Deterrence credibility
    
- Technological parity
    
- Infrastructure denial capability
    
- Continuity preservation
    
- Escalation superiority during existential conflict scenarios
    

---

# OPERATIONAL LIMITATIONS

Testing involving radiological or catastrophic yield systems shall remain geographically isolated from major Coalition civilian population centers.

NSIC shall maintain active monitoring of:

- Fallout spread
    
- Terrain contamination
    
- Structural instability
    
- Atmospheric effects
    
- Material leakage
    
- Unauthorized observation
    

Any incident exceeding acceptable containment thresholds shall trigger immediate continuity review.

---

# RECORDS NOTICE

Certain operational records associated with ATRUM SOL may exist outside conventional archival systems.

Sub-compartmentalized programs may remain undisclosed even to otherwise authorized Continuity Black personnel.

---

# CLOSING DIRECTIVE

The Coalition does not pursue these capabilities for conquest alone.

It pursues them because continuity without deterrence eventually becomes surrender.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
