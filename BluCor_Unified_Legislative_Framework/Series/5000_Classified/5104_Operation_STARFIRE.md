# PURPOSE

Operation [[5104_Operation_STARFIRE|STARFIRE]] establishes the Coalition orbital strategic systems program including surveillance, subterranean imaging, and restricted deterrence platforms.

---

# SECTION I — ORBITAL SYSTEMS

Authorized systems include:

## SUR1
Orbital imaging and reconnaissance platform.

## SUR2
Subterranean imaging and geological observation platform.

## SUR3 — [[5104_Operation_STARFIRE|STARFIRE]]
Restricted orbital deterrence platform.

---

# SECTION II — ACCESS CONTROL

Knowledge regarding:

- Launch systems
- Payload systems
- Orbital routing
- Ground uplinks
- Strategic targeting capability

remains compartmentalized.

---

# SECTION III — STRATEGIC DENIAL

Operational deployment of [[5104_Operation_STARFIRE|STARFIRE]]-linked systems must coincide with alternate visible weapons activity where feasible to preserve uncertainty regarding true strike origin.

---

# SECTION IV — CONCEALMENT

Public explanations for orbital activity may include:

- Geological research
- Atmospheric study
- Communications testing
- Mapping operations

---

# SECTION V — STRATEGIC DOCTRINE

Deterrence is strongest when adversaries cannot determine whether a capability truly exists.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

The sky becomes more valuable once weapons can remain above it.
