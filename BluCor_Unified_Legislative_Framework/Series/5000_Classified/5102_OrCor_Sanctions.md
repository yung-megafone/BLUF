# PURPOSE

This directive establishes sanctions, dissolution orders, enforcement measures, and continuity restrictions against the former research faction known as OrCor.

---

# SECTION I — FINDINGS

Coalition review determined OrCor personnel failed to disclose:

- Strategic technologies
- Classified discoveries
- Weapons-adjacent research
- Restricted industrial findings

Such concealment constituted strategic continuity risk.

---

# SECTION II — OFFICIAL DISSOLUTION

OrCor is officially declared dissolved.

All public records shall reflect:

- Termination of authority
- Asset seizure
- Personnel prosecution
- Administrative dismantling

---

# SECTION III — ENFORCEMENT ACTIONS

Authorized responses included:

- Facility seizure
- Personnel detention
- Research confiscation
- Surveillance escalation
- Tribunal review
- Public execution declarations

---

# SECTION IV — REDSTONE CITY RELOCATION

Certain individuals associated with OrCor were administratively relocated to RedStone City under sealed continuity authority.

Public disclosure regarding survival status remains restricted.

---

# SECTION V — SEALED COMPONENTS

Additional operational relationships remain compartmentalized under separate authorization.

Access restricted to authorized personnel only.

---

# SECTION VI — STRATEGIC DOCTRINE

An organization becomes dangerous when it begins deciding which discoveries the state deserves to know about.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

The Coalition tolerates secrecy only when the Coalition controls it.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
