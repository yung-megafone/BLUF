# CHARTER 1108

## WHICOR REPRESENTATION AND COMMAND CHARTER

### Governmental Authority Series

CLASSIFICATION: `L1`  
STATUS: `ACTIVE`  
AUTHORITY: `BLUCOR`  
AUTHORIZED BY: `HIGH TABLE`

---

# PURPOSE

This charter establishes [[1108_WhiCor_Representation_and_Command_Charter|WhiCor]] as the Coalition representation, liaison, and strategic coordination authority.

[[1108_WhiCor_Representation_and_Command_Charter|WhiCor]] personnel shall operate as intermediary continuity representatives between classified, administrative, military, and strategic sectors.

---

# SECTION I — RESPONSIBILITIES

[[1108_WhiCor_Representation_and_Command_Charter|WhiCor]] responsibilities include:

- Strategic representation
- Interagency coordination
- Diplomatic liaison
- Restricted communications
- Operational synchronization
- Command relay
- Clearance verification

---

# SECTION II — REPRESENTATIONAL AUTHORITY

[[1108_WhiCor_Representation_and_Command_Charter|WhiCor]] personnel may represent Coalition authority during:

- Strategic negotiations
- Classified coordination
- Infrastructure review
- Borderland operations
- Continuity planning
- Emergency administration

---

# SECTION III — ACCESS AUTHORITY

Authorized [[1108_WhiCor_Representation_and_Command_Charter|WhiCor]] personnel may receive elevated access to:

- Strategic facilities
- Classified reports
- Treasury sectors
- Orbital review summaries
- Borderland command sectors

Access remains compartmentalized.

---

# SECTION IV — STRATEGIC DOCTRINE

Coordination preserves continuity by preventing fragmentation between authorities.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

The strongest governments are not the loudest.  
They are the most coordinated.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
