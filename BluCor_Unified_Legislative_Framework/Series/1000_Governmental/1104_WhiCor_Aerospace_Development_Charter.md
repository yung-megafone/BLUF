# CHARTER 1104

## THE WHICOR AEROSPACE DEVELOPMENT CHARTER

### Enacted under the Authority of BluCor for Aerospace Advancement and Space Exploration

CLASSIFICATION: `L0 / PUBLIC`  
STATUS: `ACTIVE`  
AUTHORITY: `BLUCOR / WHICOR`  
AUTHORIZED BY: `BLUCOR LEADERSHIP`

---

> [!info] ARCHIVAL STATUS  
> This charter establishes WhiCor as the officially recognized aerospace research, exploration, and strategic aerospace maintenance organization operating under BluCor authority.

---

# Published to BWARS On

`202101121535`

---

# Article I: Founding and Purpose

## 1 - Establishment of WhiCor

WhiCor is officially designated as the Aerospace Research and Development Organization operating under the authority and oversight of BluCor.

---

## 2 - Purpose and Function

WhiCor exists to advance aerospace technologies, exploration, and innovation through:

- Research and development of all aerospace systems and technologies.
    
- Exploration, operation, and advancement of space-related endeavors.
    
- Maintenance and operational support for designated strategic aerospace sites.
    
- Advancement of long-range observational and orbital systems.
    

---

## 3 - Chain of Command

WhiCor operates as a subordinate entity to BluCor and remains accountable to the BluCor Strategization Complex (`BSC`) for:

- all activities,
    
- discoveries,
    
- aerospace operations,
    
- and technological advancements.
    

---

# Article II: Authority and Operations

## 1 - Operational Authority

WhiCor retains exclusive rights to:

- Access and utilize all aerospace above `Y150`, unless superseded by a documented property deed specifying ownership of airspace.
    
- Conduct research, testing, and space exploration initiatives.
    
- Operate and maintain ICBM sites, ensuring readiness and upkeep as directed by BluCor.
    
- Maintain designated aerospace monitoring and strategic observation infrastructure.
    

---

## 2 - Limitations of Authority

WhiCor does not retain authority to:

- Launch, control, or maneuver strategic defense equipment without explicit authorization from BluCor.
    
- Disseminate findings, discoveries, or technological advancements without passing through corresponding command structures for approval.
    
- Conduct independent warfare operations without BWARS authorization.
    

---

# Article III: Reporting and Information Control

## 1 - Mandatory Reporting

All:

- research,
    
- discoveries,
    
- findings,
    
- aerospace observations,
    
- and technological developments
    

achieved by WhiCor must be:

- documented and submitted to BluCor leadership for review,
    
- archived within the BluCor Strategization Complex for historical and operational purposes,
    
- and retained for continuity-era reference where applicable.
    

---

## 2 - Information Dissemination

BluCor retains absolute authority over the dissemination of:

- findings,
    
- discoveries,
    
- aerospace telemetry,
    
- orbital observations,
    
- and technological advancements
    

developed or acquired by WhiCor.

Unauthorized sharing, withholding, or mismanagement of information shall be treated as treasonous activity.

---

## 3 - High Treason Clause

Any:

- WhiCor researcher,
    
- affiliate,
    
- contractor,
    
- or personnel
    

found in possession of undisclosed research, findings, or technology outside approved archival or reporting systems shall be subject to:

- investigation,
    
- trial,
    
- and prosecution for High Treason under BluCor law.
    

---

# Article IV: Oversight and Compliance

## 1 - BluCor Oversight

WhiCor activities, including:

- research,
    
- exploration,
    
- aerospace operations,
    
- orbital monitoring,
    
- and strategic maintenance
    

remain subject to continuous review by BluCor leadership to ensure alignment with:

- state priorities,
    
- continuity preservation,
    
- and strategic stability.
    

---

## 2 - Compliance Enforcement

Violations of this Charter shall result in disciplinary action as determined by BluCor, up to and including prosecution for High Treason.

---

## 3 - Resource Allocation

BluCor reserves the right to:

- allocate,
    
- suspend,
    
- redirect,
    
- or restrict
    

resources provided to WhiCor based upon:

- operational performance,
    
- strategic necessity,
    
- and adherence to this Charter.
