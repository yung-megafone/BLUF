# Article I: Founding and Authority

## 1 - Establishment of BluCor

BluCor is hereby recognized as the originating and foundational entity responsible for the creation and sustenance of the Complex, including all initial energy research and developmental infrastructure.

---

## 2 - Supreme Authority

BluCor holds overarching administrative and operational authority within the Complex.

This includes the right to:

- Commission facilities, personnel, and operations.
    
- Establish, restructure, or dissolve subordinate departments or organizations without external approval.
    
- Allocate resources as deemed necessary for the stability and development of the state.
    

---

## 3 - Strategic Operations Hub

BluCor operates primarily out of the BluCor Strategization Complex (`BSC`), which serves as the nexus for:

- strategic,
    
- logistical,
    
- operational,
    
- and continuity-era decision-making.
    

---

# Article II: The Board of Warfare Alliances & Regional Stability (BWARS)

## 1 - Purpose and Structure

BWARS operates as a stabilizing alliance body under BluCor supervision.

Its primary objectives include:

- Facilitating cooperation between recognized "Cor" factions for regional security.
    
- Adjudicating requests for land acquisition, warfare initiatives, and conflict resolution.
    
- Maintaining balance and allegiance to the overall stability of the state.
    

---

## 2 - Powers of BWARS

Heads of each officially recognized "Cor" may submit petitions to:

### a.

Request land expansion or reassignment.

### b.

Initiate formal declarations of war.

### c.

Accuse individuals, factions, or entities of treasonous activities undermining state stability.

BWARS decisions require formal review and final authorization by BluCor leadership.

---

## 3 - Emergency Authority

During wartime or periods of state emergency, BWARS shall be empowered to expedite decisions related to regional security.

All such decisions remain subject to immediate oversight by BluCor leadership.

---

# Article III: BluCor Conflict Policy

## 1 - Wartime Doctrine

BluCor personnel are to operate under strict neutrality principles, supporting only the victims of aggression during wartime engagements.

BluCor reserves sole authority to determine the status of victim entities.

---

## 2 - Protection of Infrastructure and Personnel

Any act of aggression targeting:

- BluCor infrastructure,
    
- facilities,
    
- personnel,
    
- continuity systems,
    
- or strategic assets
    

is hereby declared High Treason.

Offenders shall face immediate and uncompromising response as determined by BluCor leadership.

BWARS may issue formal judgment on treason cases with BluCor retaining final authority.

---

# Article IV: Subordinate Departments

## 1 - Commissioning of Departments

BluCor retains sole authority to establish or dissolve subordinate departments, including but not limited to:

- Research Divisions
    
- Defense Sectors
    
- Energy Development Units
    
- Infrastructure Management Teams
    
- Continuity Operations Units
    
- Strategic Intelligence Offices
    

---

## 2 - Autonomy of Departments

While subordinate departments may exercise limited operational independence, they remain subject to BluCor supreme oversight and must adhere to directives issued from the Strategization Complex.

---

## 3 - Departmental Accountability

Departments are required to submit routine reports to BluCor leadership for:

- performance review,
    
- continuity compliance,
    
- and continued authorization.
    

---

# Article V: Amendments and Revisions

## 1 - Amendment Authority

BluCor retains exclusive authority to amend, revise, or expand this Charter in alignment with:

- the evolving needs of the Complex,
    
- continuity preservation,
    
- and long-term state stability.
    

---

## 2 - Documentation and Record-Keeping

All changes to this document must be:

- recorded,
    
- archived,
    
- indexed where appropriate,
    
- and made accessible within the BluCor Strategization Complex for historical and legal reference.
