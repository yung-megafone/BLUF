## THE BROCOR AGRICULTURAL CHARTER

# Article I: Founding & Purpose

## 1 - Establishment of BroCor

BroCor is officially designated as the Agriculture and Livestock Organization tasked with:

- the management,
    
- cultivation,
    
- distribution,
    
- and stewardship
    

of food production and livestock maintenance.

---

## 2 - Purpose and Function

BroCor exists to ensure sustainable agricultural practices and long-term food security through:

### A.

Cultivation of crops and farmland management.

### B.

Rearing, breeding, and oversight of livestock.

### C.

Distribution of agricultural resources for state stability and prosperity.

### D.

Preservation of agricultural continuity during periods of crisis or infrastructural instability.

---

## 3 - Independent Governance

BroCor operates as an independent entity possessing legal and operational autonomy.

While BroCor maintains binding legal and cooperative relationships with BluCor, it is not fully governed by standard BluCor administrative law outside continuity or national security circumstances.

---

# Article II: Legal Relationship with BluCor

## 1 - Legal Bindings

While BroCor maintains operational independence, it retains cooperative obligations to BluCor, including:

### A.

Adherence to state security mandates during times of crisis.

### B.

Fulfillment of agricultural quotas deemed necessary for state survival.

### C.

Collaboration on national directives pertaining to:

- food distribution,
    
- livestock deployment,
    
- and continuity-era resource stabilization.
    

---

## 2 - State Protection

BroCor personnel, assets, farmland, facilities, livestock operations, and designated agricultural territories are protected under state law.

Any:

- destruction,
    
- theft,
    
- sabotage,
    
- contamination,
    
- or unlawful seizure
    

involving BroCor property shall be prosecuted under Coalition protection statutes.

---

## 3 - Operational Autonomy

BroCor retains the right to:

### A.

Manage agricultural projects without direct oversight.

### B.

Develop independent farming or livestock initiatives.

### C.

Establish regional rules or operational procedures governing its workforce and lands.

### D.

Negotiate agricultural agreements with recognized factions where permitted under Coalition continuity doctrine.

---

# Article III: Investigatory Authority

## 1 - National Security Investigations

In the event of a national security investigation, the following conditions apply:

### A.

All BroCor lands, facilities, and operations become subject to search and investigation.

### B.

Any local or regional Bill of Rights or independent protections may be disregarded to ensure the security and continuity of the state.

---

## 2 - Scope of Searches

Searches conducted under national security directives may include:

### A.

Examination of:

- farmland,
    
- livestock operations,
    
- agricultural storage facilities,
    
- and production infrastructure.
    

### B.

Interviews, detainment, or questioning of BroCor personnel as required by BluCor security authorities.

---

## 3 - Compliance

BroCor leadership must fully comply with national security investigations and provide unrestricted access to:

- facilities,
    
- personnel,
    
- agricultural records,
    
- and logistical infrastructure
    

when ordered by BluCor authority.

---

# Article IV: Responsibilities & Oversight

## 1 - Agricultural Stewardship

BroCor is responsible for:

### A.

The maintenance, sustainability, and growth of agricultural operations.

### B.

Implementation of ethical farming and livestock practices.

### C.

Ensuring resource availability for all recognized factions and entities.

### D.

Preservation of emergency food reserves and continuity-era agricultural systems.

---

## 2 - Operational Reporting

While autonomous, BroCor must submit:

### A.

Annual reports regarding:

- food production,
    
- agricultural development,
    
- livestock population status,
    
- and regional resource conditions.
    

### B.

Emergency reports during:

- natural disasters,
    
- infestations,
    
- contamination events,
    
- or disruptions to food security infrastructure.
    

---

## 3 - State Coordination

During matters involving:

- national crisis,
    
- wartime mobilization,
    
- continuity emergencies,
    
- or widespread supply instability,
    

BluCor may assume temporary authority over BroCor assets to secure food supply chains and livestock resources.

---

# Article V: Amendments & Compliance

## 1 - Amendment Authority

This Charter may only be amended through mutual agreement between:

- BroCor leadership,
    
- and BluCor leadership.
    

---

## 2 - Noncompliance Consequences

Failure to adhere to:

- national security mandates,
    
- investigatory requests,
    
- continuity directives,
    
- or food security obligations
    

may result in penalties including:

### A.

Temporary suspension of BroCor operational autonomy.

### B.

Seizure or redistribution of agricultural resources necessary to uphold state stability.

### C.

Emergency BWARS oversight where continuity preservation requires intervention.

---

## 3 - Record Keeping

All:

- agreements,
    
- amendments,
    
- investigations,
    
- and compliance audits
    

must be documented and submitted to the BluCor Strategization Complex for archival purposes.

---

# FINAL PROVISION

Signed in Cooperative Trust and Mutual Obligation Between BroCor and BluCor Leadership.
