# CHARTER 1107

## GRCOR RESERVE AND FINANCE CHARTER

### Governmental Authority Series

CLASSIFICATION: `L1`  
STATUS: `ACTIVE`  
AUTHORITY: `BLUCOR`  
AUTHORIZED BY: `HIGH TABLE`

---

# PURPOSE

This charter establishes [[1107_GrCor_Reserve_and_Finance_Charter|GrCor]] as the Coalition reserve banking, strategic finance, and protected asset storage authority.

---

# SECTION I — RESPONSIBILITIES

[[1107_GrCor_Reserve_and_Finance_Charter|GrCor]] responsibilities include:

- Reserve storage
- Vault management
- Currency protection
- Asset accounting
- Treasury logistics
- Strategic reserve transport
- Financial continuity support

---

# SECTION II — PROTECTED STATUS

[[1107_GrCor_Reserve_and_Finance_Charter|GrCor]] facilities are designated Strategic Infrastructure.

Enhanced protections apply to:

- Vault sectors
- Underground reserves
- Treasury corridors
- Ledger archives
- Financial relay systems

---

# SECTION III — SECURITY REQUIREMENTS

[[1107_GrCor_Reserve_and_Finance_Charter|GrCor]] facilities shall maintain:

- Hardened construction
- Surveillance overlap
- [[1101_BWARS_Authority_Doctrine|BWARS]] protection
- Access logging
- Tunnel intrusion monitoring
- Emergency lockdown systems

---

# SECTION IV — STRATEGIC DOCTRINE

Reserve infrastructure exists to preserve governmental continuity during instability.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

The vault is not protecting currency alone.  
It is protecting the government's ability to continue functioning.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
