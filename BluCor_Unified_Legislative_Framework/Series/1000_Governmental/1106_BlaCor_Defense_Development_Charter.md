## THE BLACOR DEFENSE DEVELOPMENT CHARTER

# Article I: Founding and Purpose

## 1 - Establishment of BlaCor

BlaCor is officially designated as the Defense Research and Development Organization operating under the authority and oversight of BluCor.

---

## 2 - Purpose and Function

BlaCor exists to advance the state’s:

- security,
    
- technological capability,
    
- defensive preparedness,
    
- and strategic continuity infrastructure
    

through:

- Design of defense-related equipment and systems.
    
- Engineering, manufacturing, and testing of defensive tools and technologies.
    
- Ensuring readiness and strategic preparedness through continuous innovation.
    

---

## 3 - Chain of Command

BlaCor operates as a subordinate entity to BluCor and remains accountable to the BluCor Strategization Complex (`BSC`) for:

- operational conduct,
    
- resource utilization,
    
- strategic alignment,
    
- and departmental performance.
    

---

# Article II: Authority and Operations

## 1 - Operational Authority

BlaCor retains the exclusive right to:

- Conduct defense-related research and development.
    
- Engineer and manufacture defensive systems and equipment.
    
- Undertake testing and experimentation essential to the development of defense technologies.
    
- Develop continuity-oriented strategic deterrence systems where authorized.
    

---

## 2 - Commissioning Projects

All projects undertaken by BlaCor are implicitly authorized by BluCor unless explicitly stated otherwise through formal directive or BWARS restriction.

---

## 3 - Testing Protocols

BlaCor may conduct tests deemed necessary to fulfill its operational purpose, provided that:

- Testing adheres to ethical and operational safety standards.
    
- No harm, physical or otherwise, is inflicted upon individuals, property, or organizations uninvolved in the test.
    
- Strategic containment procedures remain active where required.
    

---

# Article III: Risk Management

## Definition of Risky Tests

A “risky test” is hereby defined as any:

- test,
    
- experiment,
    
- weapons trial,
    
- containment exercise,
    
- or operational procedure
    

that holds the potential to negatively impact:

- Individuals, factions, or organizations outside of BlaCor’s direct control and liability.
    
- Infrastructure, land, or resources outside of BlaCor’s designated operational zones.
    

---

## 1 - Consent Requirement

BlaCor must obtain strict consent from all parties potentially impacted by a risky test prior to the test’s initiation.

Documentation of consent must be:

- archived,
    
- indexed where appropriate,
    
- and submitted to BluCor leadership for record-keeping and oversight review.
    

---

## 2 - Accountability for Risky Tests

In the event of unplanned impact resulting from a risky test, BlaCor shall:

- Assume full liability for damages.
    
- Initiate corrective actions to mitigate harm.
    
- Submit a detailed incident report to BluCor leadership for review and oversight.
    
- Cooperate with BWARS investigative review where necessary.
    

---

# Article IV: Oversight and Reporting

## 1 - BluCor Oversight

All BlaCor activities, including:

- research,
    
- development,
    
- manufacturing,
    
- testing,
    
- and strategic experimentation
    

remain subject to review and oversight by BluCor leadership to ensure alignment with:

- state stability,
    
- continuity preservation,
    
- and regional security priorities.
    

---

## 2 - Performance Reporting

BlaCor is required to:

- Submit quarterly reports detailing:
    
    - progress,
        
    - testing outcomes,
        
    - operational requirements,
        
    - and strategic development status.
        
- Provide immediate reports regarding risky tests and incidents impacting external parties.
    

---

## 3 - Resource Allocation

BluCor retains the right to:

- allocate,
    
- reallocate,
    
- suspend,
    
- or restrict
    

resources provided to BlaCor in alignment with overarching state priorities and continuity doctrine.

---

# Article V: Amendments and Compliance

## 1 - Amendment Authority

This Charter may only be amended under the direct authority of BluCor.

---

## 2 - Compliance Mandate

Any breach of this Charter by:

- BlaCor personnel,
    
- departments,
    
- contractors,
    
- affiliates,
    
- or associated organizations
    

shall be subject to disciplinary measures determined by BluCor leadership and BWARS review structures where applicable.

---

## 3 - Record-Keeping

All:

- changes,
    
- amendments,
    
- testing consents,
    
- and incident reports
    

must be documented and archived within the BluCor Strategization Complex.
