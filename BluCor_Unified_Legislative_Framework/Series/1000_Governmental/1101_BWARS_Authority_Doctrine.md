# PURPOSE

This charter establishes the authority, jurisdiction, operational scope, and continuity responsibilities of the Bureau of Warfare, Administration, Regulation, and Security (BWARS).

BWARS shall function as the Coalition’s primary visible enforcement and stability preservation authority.

---

# SECTION I — CORE RESPONSIBILITIES

BWARS responsibilities include:

- Civil enforcement
- Infrastructure security
- Checkpoint operations
- Border patrol
- Riot suppression
- Hazard response assistance
- Strategic facility protection
- Population stabilization
- Tribunal enforcement
- Curfew enforcement

---

# SECTION II — JURISDICTION

BWARS jurisdiction extends throughout all Coalition territory including:

- Cities
- Borderlands
- Underground sectors
- Rail systems
- Treasury facilities
- Strategic infrastructure
- Missile testing regions
- Restricted administrative sectors

---

# SECTION III — EMERGENCY AUTHORITY

During Continuity Emergencies, BWARS may:

- Restrict movement
- Establish checkpoints
- Seal districts
- Conduct detainment
- Enforce curfew
- Assist evacuations
- Protect continuity assets

---

# SECTION IV — FORCE AUTHORIZATION

BWARS personnel may utilize force proportional to:

- Threat level
- Strategic importance
- Infrastructure risk
- Civil instability
- Continuity danger

Lethal force may be authorized within restricted zones.

---

# SECTION V — STRATEGIC DOCTRINE

BWARS exists not merely to enforce law, but to preserve continuity through visible authority.
