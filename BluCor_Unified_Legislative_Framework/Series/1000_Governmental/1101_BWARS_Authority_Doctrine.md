# PURPOSE

This charter establishes the authority, jurisdiction, operational scope, and continuity responsibilities of the Bureau of Warfare, Administration, Regulation, and Security ([[1101_BWARS_Authority_Doctrine|BWARS]]).

[[1101_BWARS_Authority_Doctrine|BWARS]] shall function as the Coalition’s primary visible enforcement and stability preservation authority.

---

# SECTION I — CORE RESPONSIBILITIES

[[1101_BWARS_Authority_Doctrine|BWARS]] responsibilities include:

- Civil enforcement
- Infrastructure security
- Checkpoint operations
- Border patrol
- Riot suppression
- Hazard response assistance
- Strategic facility protection
- Population stabilization
- Tribunal enforcement
- Curfew enforcement

---

# SECTION II — JURISDICTION

[[1101_BWARS_Authority_Doctrine|BWARS]] jurisdiction extends throughout all Coalition territory including:

- Cities
- Borderlands
- Underground sectors
- Rail systems
- Treasury facilities
- Strategic infrastructure
- Missile testing regions
- Restricted administrative sectors

---

# SECTION III — EMERGENCY AUTHORITY

During Continuity Emergencies, [[1101_BWARS_Authority_Doctrine|BWARS]] may:

- Restrict movement
- Establish checkpoints
- Seal districts
- Conduct detainment
- Enforce curfew
- Assist evacuations
- Protect continuity assets

---

# SECTION IV — FORCE AUTHORIZATION

[[1101_BWARS_Authority_Doctrine|BWARS]] personnel may utilize force proportional to:

- Threat level
- Strategic importance
- Infrastructure risk
- Civil instability
- Continuity danger

Lethal force may be authorized within restricted zones.

---

# SECTION V — STRATEGIC DOCTRINE

[[1101_BWARS_Authority_Doctrine|BWARS]] exists not merely to enforce law, but to preserve continuity through visible authority.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

A government becomes real when its authority can physically arrive.
