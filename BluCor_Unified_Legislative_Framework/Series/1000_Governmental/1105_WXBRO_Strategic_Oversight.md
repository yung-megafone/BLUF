# PURPOSE

This charter establishes the authority, oversight role, and continuity responsibilities of WXBRO personnel.

WXBRO personnel represent elevated continuity oversight authority operating above standard administrative structure.

---

# SECTION I — RESPONSIBILITIES

WXBRO responsibilities include:

- Strategic review
- Continuity oversight
- Classified directive authorization
- Emergency coordination
- Interagency synchronization
- Strategic containment review
- Orbital oversight
- Continuity succession management

---

# SECTION II — COMPARTMENTALIZATION

WXBRO operations may remain:

- Need-to-know restricted
- Partially undisclosed
- Compartmentalized
- Operationally deniable

---

# SECTION III — OVERSIGHT AUTHORITY

WXBRO personnel may review:

- NSIC operations
- Treasury continuity
- BWARS escalation
- Strategic missile systems
- Hazard containment
- Orbital deterrence systems

---

# SECTION IV — CONTINUITY PRIORITY

WXBRO authority exists solely to preserve continuity under conditions where ordinary administration becomes insufficient.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

The highest authority is the authority responsible for what happens after failure.
