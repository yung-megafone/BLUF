# CHARTER 1106

## BLUCOR CENTRAL ADMINISTRATION CHARTER

### Governmental Authority Series

CLASSIFICATION: `L1`  
STATUS: `ACTIVE`  
AUTHORITY: `BLUCOR`  
AUTHORIZED BY: `HIGH TABLE`

---

# PURPOSE

This charter establishes BluCor Central Administration as the primary administrative governance structure of the Coalition.

---

# SECTION I — RESPONSIBILITIES

BluCor Central Administration oversees:

- District governance
- Administrative coordination
- Civic registration
- Resource distribution
- Infrastructure oversight
- Public communications
- Internal policy synchronization

---

# SECTION II — ADMINISTRATIVE AUTHORITY

BluCor may establish:

- District offices
- Administrative sectors
- Public works divisions
- Registry systems
- Population databases
- Compliance offices

---

# SECTION III — STRATEGIC ROLE

BluCor administration serves as the visible governing framework maintaining operational order across Coalition territory.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

A state survives longer when administration becomes routine.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
