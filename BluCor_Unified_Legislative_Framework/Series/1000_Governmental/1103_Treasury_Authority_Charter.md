# CHARTER 1103

## TREASURY AUTHORITY CHARTER

### Governmental Authority Series

CLASSIFICATION: `L1`  
STATUS: `ACTIVE`  
AUTHORITY: `BLUCOR`  
AUTHORIZED BY: `HIGH TABLE`

---

# PURPOSE

This charter establishes the Coalition Treasury Authority as the centralized financial governance and reserve management authority of the [[1106_BluCor_Central_Administration_Charter|BluCor]] Strategic Coalition.

---

# SECTION I — RESPONSIBILITIES

Treasury Authority responsibilities include:

- Currency regulation
- Reserve management
- Strategic resource accounting
- Infrastructure funding
- Emergency allocation
- Economic stabilization
- Reserve bank oversight

---

# SECTION II — RESERVE AUTHORITY

The Treasury Authority maintains exclusive oversight over:

- Reserve vaults
- Strategic assets
- Currency conversion
- Resource valuation
- Emergency reserves
- Protected financial infrastructure

---

# SECTION III — SECURITY

Treasury infrastructure shall maintain:

- Hardened vaults
- Surveillance integration
- [[1101_BWARS_Authority_Doctrine|BWARS]] protection
- Underground redundancy
- Restricted access
- Emergency lockdown systems

---

# SECTION IV — EMERGENCY POWERS

During emergencies, Treasury Authority may:

- Freeze assets
- Restrict withdrawals
- Redirect resources
- Requisition supplies
- Suspend exchanges
- Nationalize infrastructure

---

# SECTION V — STRATEGIC DOCTRINE

A treasury is not merely economic infrastructure.

It is continuity stored in physical form.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

A government survives longer when its reserves survive longer.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
