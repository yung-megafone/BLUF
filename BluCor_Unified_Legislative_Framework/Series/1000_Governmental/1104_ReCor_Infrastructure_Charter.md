# CHARTER 1104

## RECOR INFRASTRUCTURE CHARTER

### Governmental Authority Series

CLASSIFICATION: `L1`  
STATUS: `ACTIVE`  
AUTHORITY: `BLUCOR`  
AUTHORIZED BY: `HIGH TABLE`

---

# PURPOSE

This charter establishes [[1104_ReCor_Infrastructure_Charter|ReCor]] as the Coalition authority responsible for infrastructure construction, urban development, housing expansion, and continuity-compatible structural planning.

---

# SECTION I — RESPONSIBILITIES

[[1104_ReCor_Infrastructure_Charter|ReCor]] responsibilities include:

- Urban planning
- Residential development
- Underground expansion
- Transportation construction
- Public works
- Structural hardening
- Emergency shelter integration

---

# SECTION II — STRATEGIC DEVELOPMENT

All major [[1104_ReCor_Infrastructure_Charter|ReCor]] projects shall consider:

- Surveillance compatibility
- Defensive integration
- Continuity fallback capability
- Emergency relocation access
- Underground expansion potential

---

# SECTION III — RESTRICTED CONSTRUCTION

[[1104_ReCor_Infrastructure_Charter|ReCor]] may restrict, seize, redesign, or demolish structures threatening:

- Infrastructure continuity
- Strategic access
- Transportation flow
- Security visibility
- Hazard containment

---

# SECTION IV — STRATEGIC DOCTRINE

Infrastructure is governance made physical.

Every structure changes territorial authority.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

A city is easier to govern when it was designed to be governed.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
