# ARTICLE 69

## SUBSTANCE-RELATED CRIMES

### Coalition Penal Enforcement Statutes

CLASSIFICATION: `L0`  
STATUS: `ACTIVE`  
AUTHORITY: `BLUCOR / BWARS JUDICIAL AUTHORITY`

---

> [!warning] LEGAL NOTICE  
> The following statutes govern:
> 
> - intoxicants,
>     
> - precursor substances,
>     
> - unauthorized cultivation,
>     
> - narcotics distribution,
>     
> - and chemical synthesis operations
>     
> 
> under Coalition penal doctrine.

---

# § 69-1 Possession of Intoxicants

## Charge

Possession of any intoxicants, including but not limited to:

- controlled substances,
    
- narcotics,
    
- prohibited compounds,
    
- or precursor materials
    

when such materials may be readily combined or utilized to produce an intoxicant.

---

## Punishment

- Up to `10 years` confinement within Tyrant’s Refuge MS.
    
- Fines equal to the assessed market value of the intoxicants.
    
- Confiscation of all illegal materials and associated paraphernalia.
    

---

# § 69-2 Influence of Intoxicants

## Charge

Being under the influence of any intoxicant or precursor substance while occupying:

- a position of governance,
    
- operational authority,
    
- security oversight,
    
- or critical responsibility
    

where intoxication impairs:

- judgment,
    
- decision-making,
    
- or fulfillment of assigned duties.
    

---

## Punishment

- Up to `5 years` confinement within Tyrant’s Refuge MS.
    
- Mandatory rehabilitation and reeducation procedures.
    
- Suspension of rights to:
    
    - hold office,
        
    - exercise authority,
        
    - or perform critical operational duties
        

for up to `5 years`.

---

# § 69-3 Unlicensed Hemp Production

## Charge

Unauthorized production, cultivation, or processing of hemp without:

- proper certification,
    
- Charter authorization,
    
- or approved Coalition agricultural licensing.
    

---

## Punishment

- Minimum `5 years` confinement within Tyrant’s Refuge MS.
    
- Confiscation of:
    
    - materials,
        
    - agricultural equipment,
        
    - and production infrastructure.
        
- Fines equal to `100%` of the value of seized materials.
    

---

# § 69-4 Unauthorized Marijuana Production

## Charge

Cultivation or production of marijuana outside the direct approval and oversight of:

- BluCor,
    
- BWARS authority,
    
- or an authorized faction possessing proper certification and legal authority.
    

---

## Punishment

- Minimum `15 years` confinement within Tyrant’s Refuge MS.
    
- Confiscation of all illegal materials and associated infrastructure.
    
- Fines equal to `300%` of the value of produced materials.
    
- Additional prosecution pursuant to § 71-1 where evidence indicates:
    
    - harm to state security,
        
    - organized trafficking,
        
    - or destabilization of Coalition order.
        

---

# § 69-5 Distribution of Intoxicants

## Charge

Illegal distribution of intoxicants including:

- trafficking,
    
- transportation,
    
- sale,
    
- exchange,
    
- or organized dissemination
    

of unauthorized substances.

---

## Punishment

- Up to `20 years` confinement within Tyrant’s Refuge MS.
    
- Confiscation of:
    
    - illegal goods,
        
    - transport vehicles,
        
    - currency,
        
    - and assets associated with distribution operations.
        
- Extensive Treasury penalties proportionate to the scale of distribution.
    

---

# § 69-6 Manufacturing or Chemical Synthesis of Intoxicants

## Charge

Manufacturing, synthesizing, refining, or chemically producing intoxicants or precursor compounds for:

- distribution,
    
- trafficking,
    
- stockpiling,
    
- or personal use.
    

---

## Punishment

- Up to `25 years` confinement within Tyrant’s Refuge MS.
    
- Confiscation of:
    
    - laboratories,
        
    - production facilities,
        
    - equipment,
        
    - and synthesized compounds.
        
- Fines equal to `300%` of the assessed value of produced intoxicants or precursor materials.
