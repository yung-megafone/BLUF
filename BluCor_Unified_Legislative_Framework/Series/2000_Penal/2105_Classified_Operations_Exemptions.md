# PURPOSE

This directive establishes legal exemptions and operational protections for authorized classified operations conducted in preservation of Coalition continuity.

---

# SECTION I — EXEMPT OPERATIONS

Authorized classified operations may receive exemption from standard:

- Reporting requirements
- Disclosure obligations
- Administrative review
- Public oversight
- Territorial disclosure
- Operational transparency

---

# SECTION II — AUTHORIZED AUTHORITIES

Exemptions may only be authorized by:

- High Table
- WXBRO oversight
- NSIC command
- Sealed continuity authority

---

# SECTION III — LIMITATIONS

Exemptions shall exist solely for:

- Continuity preservation
- Strategic deterrence
- Counterintelligence
- Infrastructure protection
- Classified containment
- Operational secrecy

Personal enrichment does not qualify as continuity purpose.

---

# SECTION IV — PUBLIC NONDISCLOSURE

Certain operations may:

- Remain denied publicly
- Receive altered public explanation
- Be removed from official records
- Operate under false designation

Operational concealment may continue permanently.

---

# SECTION V — LIABILITY PROTECTION

Authorized personnel acting under classified continuity authority may receive exemption from ordinary prosecution where actions were conducted under sealed operational mandate.

Improper authorization voids protection.

---

# SECTION VI — STRATEGIC DOCTRINE

Some operations protect continuity specifically because the public never learns they occurred.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

The existence of an operation and the existence of a record are not the same thing.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
