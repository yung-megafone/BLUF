# ARTICLE 71

>

---

# § 71-1 Economic Crimes

## Charge

Committing economic crimes including but not limited to:

### A.

Forgery or counterfeit production involving:

- currency,
    
- Treasury notes,
    
- exchange certificates,
    
- or official Treasury documentation.
    

### B.

Participation in unauthorized trade practices resulting in:

- economic instability,
    
- Treasury disruption,
    
- market manipulation,
    
- or harm to Coalition continuity systems.
    

---

## Punishment

- Up to `10 years` confinement within Tyrant’s Refuge MS.
    
- Confiscation of all profits or assets acquired through unlawful activity.
    
- Extensive Treasury fines including restitution of:
    
    - fraudulent materials,
        
    - counterfeit instruments,
        
    - and unlawfully acquired profits.
        

---

# § 71-2 Felony Theft or Vandalism

## Charge

Theft, sabotage, unlawful seizure, or willful destruction involving:

- BluCor property,
    
- Treasury property,
    
- Reserve Bank infrastructure,
    
- continuity facilities,
    
- or protected Coalition assets.
    

---

## Punishment

- Up to `15 years` confinement within Tyrant’s Refuge MS.
    
- Full restitution equal to the value of damaged or stolen property.
    
- Enhanced sentencing for repeat or aggravated offenses.
    
- Maximum penalties of up to `25 years` imprisonment for severe or organized violations.
    

---

# § 71-3 Failure to Maintain Secure Detention Facilities

## Charge

Operation or maintenance of inadequate detention infrastructure resulting in:

- escape,
    
- unlawful release,
    
- injury,
    
- or security compromise.
    

Negligence includes but is not limited to:

- structural failure,
    
- inadequate staffing,
    
- improper security procedures,
    
- or operational misconduct.
    

---

## Punishment

- Imprisonment for no less than `5 years`.
    
- Severe Treasury fines proportionate to the level of negligence.
    
- Suspension of operational authority for violating organizations or facilities.
    
- Mandatory corrective oversight where ordered by BWARS authority.
    

---

# § 71-4 Allowing Escape or Premature Release of Detainees

## Charge

Allowing detainees to:

- escape,
    
- evade confinement,
    
- or obtain release
    

without proper:

- judicial authorization,
    
- BWARS approval,
    
- or lawful release procedure.
    

---

## Punishment

- Imprisonment for no less than `10 years`.
    
- Treasury fines covering:
    
    - recapture operations,
        
    - continuity disruption,
        
    - and damages resulting from the breach.
        
- Revocation of authority or removal from positions involving:
    
    - governance,
        
    - detention oversight,
        
    - judicial authority,
        
    - or law enforcement operations.
