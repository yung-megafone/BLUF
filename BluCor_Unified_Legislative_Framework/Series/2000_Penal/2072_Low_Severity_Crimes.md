# ARTICLE 72

## LOW SEVERITY CRIMES

CLASSIFICATION: `L0  
STATUS: `ACTIVE`  
AUTHORITY: `BLUCOR / BWARS JUDICIAL AUTHORITY`

> 
> - misdemeanor offenses,
>     
> - public safety infractions,
>     
> - records violations,
>     
> - and failures of civic reporting
>     
> 
> under Coalition judicial doctrine.

---

# § 72-1 Misdemeanors Related to Public Safety

## Charge

Minor infractions involving:

- public safety,
    
- civil order,
    
- operational negligence,
    
- or conduct disruptive to Coalition stability.
    

Violations may include:

- unsafe conduct,
    
- unlawful public disruption,
    
- minor regulatory violations,
    
- or negligent interference with protected infrastructure.
    

---

## Punishment

- Up to `1 year` confinement within Tyrant’s Refuge MS or assignment to community service operations.
    
- Treasury restitution or fines equal to up to `50%` of the value of exchanged or affected materials.
    
- Probationary restrictions or suspension of trading rights for up to `5 years`.
    

---

# § 72-2 Destruction of Official Documents

## Charge

Unauthorized:

- destruction,
    
- alteration,
    
- concealment,
    
- withholding,
    
- or manipulation
    

of:

- state records,
    
- Treasury documents,
    
- judicial archives,
    
- continuity reports,
    
- or official Coalition records.
    

---

## Punishment

- Up to `5 years` confinement within Tyrant’s Refuge MS.
    
- Mandatory restitution and archival restoration requirements.
    
- Compulsory public service involving:
    
    - records management,
        
    - archival restoration,
        
    - or continuity documentation operations.
        

---

# § 72-3 Misdemeanor Failure to Report Violations

## Charge

Failure to report:

- economic violations,
    
- Treasury manipulation,
    
- criminal activity,
    
- continuity breaches,
    
- or unlawful operations
    

when such activity is reasonably known to the individual.

---

## Punishment

- Up to `2 years` confinement within Tyrant’s Refuge MS.
    
- Temporary suspension or reduction of:
    
    - civil privileges,
        
    - trading rights,
        
    - or public office eligibility.
        
- Mandatory compliance review where ordered by BWARS authority.
