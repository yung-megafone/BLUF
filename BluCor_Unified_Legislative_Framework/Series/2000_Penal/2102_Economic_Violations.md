# DIRECTIVE 2102

## ECONOMIC VIOLATIONS

### Penal and Judicial Series

CLASSIFICATION: `L0  
STATUS: `ACTIVE`  
AUTHORITY: `BLUCOR / BWARS`  
AUTHORIZED BY: `HIGH TABLE`

---

# PURPOSE

This directive establishes offenses relating to economic disruption, reserve interference, unauthorized trade, and financial destabilization.

---

# SECTION I — PROHIBITED ACTIVITY

Economic Violations include:

- Counterfeiting
- Reserve theft
- Unauthorized currency issuance
- Resource hoarding
- Black market trade
- Strategic material concealment
- Illegal conversion operations
- Financial fraud
- Treasury sabotage
- Trade manipulation

---

# SECTION II — STRATEGIC MATERIALS

Unauthorized possession or sale of:

- Yellorium
- Reactor byproducts
- Classified compounds
- Restricted industrial components
- Strategic fuels

may constitute Strategic Economic Interference.

---

# SECTION III — TREASURY INTERFERENCE

Interference involving:

- Reserve vaults
- Treasury records
- Underground vault systems
- Currency transfers
- Reserve transport

shall receive enhanced penalties.

---

# SECTION IV — ENFORCEMENT

[[1300_Treasury_Authority_Charter|Treasury Authority]] and [[1101_BWARS_Authority_Doctrine|BWARS]] may:

- Freeze assets
- Seize materials
- Conduct audits
- Suspend trade
- Restrict movement
- Detain suspects

---

# SECTION V — STRATEGIC DOCTRINE

Economic stability is continuity infrastructure.

Financial destabilization threatens governance as effectively as physical attack.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

A government collapses faster when its currency loses meaning.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
