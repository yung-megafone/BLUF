# § 77-1 Adherence to the BULF

## Mandate

All:

- employees,
    
- contractors,
    
- affiliates,
    
- advisors,
    
- and operational personnel
    

operating under BluCor authority shall maintain strict adherence to the:

```text
BluCor Unified Legislative Framework (BULF)
```

---

## Violation Consequences

Any violation of the BULF committed by BluCor personnel shall constitute:

```text
Guilty for Egregious Treason
```

subject to continuity judicial review.

---

## Punishment

Authorized penalties include:

- mandatory death penalty,
    
- or indefinite incarceration (`INDEF`)  
    within Tyrant’s Refuge MS.
    

---

# § 77-2 Standards of Conduct

## Charge

Failure to maintain the required standards of:

- loyalty,
    
- operational discipline,
    
- integrity,
    
- secrecy,
    
- or duty
    

expected of BluCor personnel.

This includes:

- negligence,
    
- misconduct,
    
- operational unreliability,
    
- or conduct harmful to continuity authority.
    

---

## Punishment

- Immediate termination of employment.
    
- Forfeiture of all employment-related privileges and benefits.
    
- Minimum imprisonment term of `10 years` within Tyrant’s Refuge MS.
    
- Additional compliance review where ordered by Intelligence and Compliance authorities.
    

---

# § 77-3 Disclosure of Sensitive Information

## Charge

Unauthorized:

- disclosure,
    
- dissemination,
    
- mishandling,
    
- theft,
    
- duplication,
    
- or retention
    

of:

- classified information,
    
- sensitive records,
    
- continuity materials,
    
- operational intelligence,
    
- or restricted BluCor doctrine.
    

---

## Punishment

- Immediate designation of:
    

```text
Guilty for Egregious Treason
```

- Confiscation of all assets, property, and operational privileges.
    
- Indefinite incarceration (`INDEF`) within Tyrant’s Refuge MS.
    
- Expanded investigation of:
    
    - affiliates,
        
    - communication history,
        
    - and associated personnel.
        

---

# § 77-4 Unauthorized Absence

## Charge

Failure to:

- report for duty,
    
- maintain operational presence,
    
- or remain at an assigned post
    

without proper authorization or continuity clearance.

This includes:

- abandonment of assignment,
    
- desertion,
    
- unexplained absence,
    
- or unauthorized withdrawal from operational responsibilities.
    

---

## Punishment

- Imprisonment for up to `20 years` within Tyrant’s Refuge MS.
    
- Immediate loss of employment and operational authority.
    
- Confiscation of assets where ordered by judicial review.
    
- Restriction from future employment involving continuity operations.
    

---

# § 77-5 Acts of Defiance or Insurrection

## Charge

Acts involving:

- rebellion,
    
- insubordination,
    
- refusal of lawful orders,
    
- organized resistance,
    
- sabotage,
    
- or defiance
    

against:

- BluCor authority,
    
- continuity governance,
    
- strategic leadership,
    
- or operational command structures.
    

---

## Punishment

- Immediate designation of:
    

```text
Guilty for Egregious Treason
```

- Mandatory death penalty or indefinite incarceration (`INDEF`) within Tyrant’s Refuge MS.
    
- Confiscation of all state-issued property and operational materials.
    
- Expanded scrutiny upon all known associates and affiliated personnel.
