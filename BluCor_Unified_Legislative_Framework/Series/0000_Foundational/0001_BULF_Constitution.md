# DIRECTIVE 0001

## BULF CONSTITUTION

### Foundational Series

CLASSIFICATION: `L0`  
STATUS: `ACTIVE`  
AUTHORITY: `BLUCOR`  
AUTHORIZED BY: `HIGH TABLE`

---

# PURPOSE

This directive establishes the [[1106_BluCor_Central_Administration_Charter|BluCor]] Unified Legislative Framework Constitution as the supreme governing authority of the [[1106_BluCor_Central_Administration_Charter|BluCor]] Strategic Coalition.

All agencies, charters, penal statutes, classified directives, regulatory frameworks, military doctrines, and public authorities derive legitimacy from this Constitution.

---

# PREAMBLE

We, the recognized governing authorities of the BluCor Strategic Coalition, acting in preservation of territorial continuity, public order, infrastructure stability, economic regulation, strategic defense, and governmental survival, hereby enact this Constitution as the supreme law of Coalition territory.

The Coalition recognizes that civilization survives only where authority, infrastructure, and continuity remain intact.

Liberty may exist only where order permits it.

---

# SECTION I — FOUNDATIONAL AUTHORITY

## §1.1 Foundational Era

The Coalition recognizes 2020-03-21 as the beginning of the Foundational Era.

All lands, settlements, roads, structures, underground systems, industrial sectors, and strategic developments established during and after this era may be recognized as part of Coalition continuity territory.

---

## §1.2 Establishment of BULF

The BluCor Unified Legislative Framework, established circa 2024 and constitutionally ratified on 2026-05-23, shall serve as the supreme legal framework of the Coalition.

BULF supersedes conflicting local, factional, corporate, district, or autonomous authority.

---

## §1.3 Constitutional Supremacy

No directive, charter, policy, tribunal ruling, military order, or public statement may override this Constitution unless authorized under emergency continuity provisions.

---

# SECTION II — GOVERNING STRUCTURE

## §2.1 The Coalition

The BluCor Strategic Coalition is recognized as the central governing authority over all Coalition-controlled territory.

The Coalition shall preserve:

- Territorial sovereignty
- Infrastructure continuity
- Public order
- Strategic deterrence
- Economic stability
- Intelligence security
- Legal uniformity

---

## §2.2 The High Table

The High Table shall serve as the supreme continuity and constitutional review authority.

The High Table may:

- Ratify constitutional amendments
- Approve classified directives
- Declare continuity emergencies
- Authorize agency charters
- Interpret constitutional disputes
- Review strategic operations

Proceedings may remain classified where disclosure threatens continuity.

---

## §2.3 Authorized Agencies

The Coalition may charter agencies, ministries, bureaus, commissions, and authorities including but not limited to:

- [[1101_BWARS_Authority_Doctrine|BWARS]]
- [[1102_NSIC_Operational_Authority|NSIC]]
- [[1104_ReCor_Infrastructure_Charter|ReCor]]
- [[1107_GrCor_Reserve_and_Finance_Charter|GrCor]]
- [[1108_WhiCor_Representation_and_Command_Charter|WhiCor]]
- [[1103_Treasury_Authority_Charter|Treasury Authority]]
- [[1105_WXBRO_Strategic_Oversight|WXBRO]] command elements

Agency powers shall be defined by charter.

---

# SECTION III — CITIZENS AND RESIDENTS

## §3.1 Conditional Civic Status

Citizens and residents may enjoy protections, services, and privileges under Coalition law provided they remain compliant with Coalition authority.

Civic status may be restricted, suspended, or revoked under lawful process, tribunal authority, or continuity emergency powers.

---

## §3.2 Civic Duties

All citizens and residents shall:

- Obey Coalition law
- Maintain identification
- Report security threats
- Cooperate with lawful inspections
- Preserve infrastructure
- Comply with emergency orders
- Avoid unauthorized contact with hostile factions

Failure to perform civic duty may result in administrative restriction or criminal liability.

---

# SECTION IV — RIGHTS AND LIMITATIONS

## §4.1 Recognized Rights

The Coalition recognizes conditional rights including:

- Shelter
- Food access
- Water access
- Labor participation
- Medical access
- Petition
- Limited expression
- Limited movement
- Due process

These rights are further defined under Directive [[0002_Bill_of_Rights|0002]].

---

## §4.2 Limitation Clause

No right shall be interpreted to permit:

- Treason
- Sabotage
- Disorder
- Unauthorized disclosure
- Infrastructure interference
- Strategic destabilization
- Hostile propaganda
- Threats to continuity

Continuity supersedes individual privilege.

---

# SECTION V — TERRITORY AND INFRASTRUCTURE

## §5.1 Territorial Authority

Coalition jurisdiction extends across all recognized districts, facilities, underground systems, borderlands, strategic sites, and administrative sectors.

---

## §5.2 Strategic Infrastructure

Strategic infrastructure includes:

- Treasury facilities
- Rail systems
- Power systems
- Surveillance networks
- Communications arrays
- Underground corridors
- Detention facilities
- Orbital uplink sites
- Hazard containment sites

Strategic infrastructure may receive enhanced legal protection.

---

# SECTION VI — LAW AND TRIBUNALS

## §6.1 Legal Authority

The Coalition may establish criminal codes, economic statutes, regulatory frameworks, tribunal systems, and classified legal exemptions.

---

## §6.2 Tribunal Authority

Military tribunals may supersede civilian process where continuity, classified operations, strategic infrastructure, or security threats are involved.

---

# SECTION VII — CLASSIFIED OPERATIONS

## §7.1 Classified Authority

The Coalition may conduct classified operations where secrecy is required to preserve continuity.

Such operations may remain undisclosed indefinitely.

---

## §7.2 Public Narrative Control

Public statements may omit, simplify, delay, or redirect operational details where full disclosure would threaten stability.

---

# SECTION VIII — AMENDMENTS

## §8.1 Amendment Authority

This Constitution may be amended by High Table approval.

Emergency amendments may take immediate effect during continuity emergencies.

---

## §8.2 Permanent Principles

No amendment may abolish:

- Coalition continuity authority
- Centralized territorial sovereignty
- Strategic infrastructure protection
- Classified operation authority
- Emergency powers
- Constitutional supremacy

---

# CLOSING DOCTRINE

The Coalition exists because collapse is always possible.

Law preserves order.
Order preserves infrastructure.
Infrastructure preserves civilization.
Civilization preserves continuity.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

The Constitution is not a promise of comfort.  
It is a mechanism of survival.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
