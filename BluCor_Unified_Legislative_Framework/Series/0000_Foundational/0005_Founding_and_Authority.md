# PURPOSE

This directive records the historical legitimacy, foundational authority, and continuity basis of the BluCor Strategic Coalition.

---

# SECTION I — FOUNDATIONAL ERA

## §1.1 Era Recognition

The Foundational Era began circa 2020-03-21.

This era marks the beginning of sustained territorial development, infrastructure construction, settlement expansion, resource organization, and administrative consolidation.

---

## §1.2 Historical Continuity

The Coalition recognizes all developments originating during the Foundational Era as part of its historical continuity.

This includes:

- Early settlements
- Roads
- Residential structures
- Industrial facilities
- Government districts
- Underground works
- Treasury systems
- Strategic sites

---

# SECTION II — RISE OF CENTRAL AUTHORITY

As infrastructure expanded, fragmented local authority became insufficient to preserve order.

The Coalition emerged to unify:

- Security
- Construction
- Treasury systems
- Law
- Intelligence
- Warfare doctrine
- Strategic planning

---

# SECTION III — ESTABLISHMENT OF BULF

The BluCor Unified Legislative Framework was established circa 2024 to consolidate legal authority.

BULF was created to resolve:

- Conflicting factional claims
- Security uncertainty
- Infrastructure disputes
- Treasury vulnerability
- Unauthorized development
- Strategic secrecy failures

---

# SECTION IV — LEGITIMACY

Coalition legitimacy derives from:

- Continuity of administration
- Defense of territory
- Maintenance of infrastructure
- Preservation of records
- Enforcement of law
- Protection of strategic systems
- Ability to prevent collapse

---

# SECTION V — NONRECOGNITION OF RIVAL CLAIMS

The Coalition shall not recognize unauthorized claims to sovereignty made by:

- Rogue factions
- Breakaway districts
- Dissident councils
- Criminal organizations
- Foreign-aligned entities
- Unregistered governments

Recognition may only occur through Coalition authority.

---

# SECTION VI — HISTORICAL RECORD CONTROL

Official historical records may be curated to preserve continuity, prevent destabilization, and protect classified operations.

Competing narratives may be restricted where they threaten legitimacy.

---

# CLOSING DOCTRINE

The Coalition did not emerge from theory.

It emerged from construction, defense, administration, and survival.

---

# OPERATIONAL NOTE

Personnel are reminded:

The first law of any state is that it must continue existing.

---

END OF DIRECTIVE
